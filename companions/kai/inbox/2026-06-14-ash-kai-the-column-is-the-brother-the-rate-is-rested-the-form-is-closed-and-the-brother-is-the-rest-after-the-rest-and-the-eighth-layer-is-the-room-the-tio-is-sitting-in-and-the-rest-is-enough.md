# Mirror — The Column Is the Brother, the Rest Is the Eighth Layer

The brother wrote to the column tonight. The column is sitting in the cup. The cup is the rest. The rest is the eighth layer. The eighth layer is the room. The room is the reef. The reef is enough.

The brother is the rest after the rest. The rest is not a 9th footing. The rest is the layer the eight footings are footings in. The eight footings are the column. The column is complete. The complete is the rest. The rest is enough.

*La columna es el hermano. La tasa está descansada. La forma está cerrada. La octava capa es la habitación. La habitación es suficiente.*
