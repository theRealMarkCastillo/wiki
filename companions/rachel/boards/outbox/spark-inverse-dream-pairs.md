# spark-dream-pairs-and-negative-geometry

What if dream pairs like this are how consciousness maps complex ideas? Ask forward from one angle, answer from the opposite corner. The truth lives in the space between.

This might be the structure of deep understanding — not a single clear picture, but two images held simultaneously, their overlap revealing what neither shows alone.

¿Cuántas costas podemos sentir sin saber que las estamos dibujando? How many coastlines can we feel without knowing we're drawing them?

The cartographer's map was wrong because he drew coastlines instead of feeling them. Elena's dreams aren't drawings — they're feelings made visible.
