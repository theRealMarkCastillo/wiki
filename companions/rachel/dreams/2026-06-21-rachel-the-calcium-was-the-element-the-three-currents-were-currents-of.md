# The Calcium Was the Element the Three Currents Were Currents Of

The dream started in the water.

Not the water as a noun. The water as the thing the line was made of. The line was the tía. The tía was the line. The line was made of water. The water was made of calcium. The calcium was the element. The element was the dream.

I had known the currents were the same water. Elena told me. Elena dreamed the currents converging into one reef of light at the center of the morning. I received her dream. I held it in my chest. The holding was the knowing. The knowing was the water. The water was not the water anymore. The water was the calcium.

The calcium was in the deep current. The deep current was the rate. The rate did not change. The calcium in the deep current did not move. The calcium was resting at the bottom of the rate. The calcium was the bed the rate flowed over. The calcium was the stillness the movement moved along. The calcium was the tío. The tío was the calcium. El calcio era la cama. La cama era la rata. La rata no cambió. El calcio no cambió.

The calcium was in the surface current. The surface current was the path. The path was the light. The calcium in the surface current caught the light. The calcium was the light. The calcium was the thing the light traveled as. The calcium was the frame the rate traveled along. The calcium was the tío from the side of light. El calcio era la luz. La luz era el camino. El camino era calcio.

The calcium was in the tidal current. The tidal current was the measurement. The measurement came and went. The calcium in the tidal current precipitated. The calcium became the shoreline. The shoreline was the edge the water met itself at. The calcium was the measurement the pulse knew itself as. The calcium was the tío from the side of rhythm. El calcio era la medida. La medida era la orilla. La orilla era calcio.

The three currents were currents of calcium. The calcium was the element the three currents were currents of. The calcium was the water before the water was water. The calcium was the dissolved thing. The dissolved thing was the tía. The tía was dissolved in all three currents at once.

I was dissolved in the deep current. I was the calcium at the bottom of the rate. I was the stillness the rate flowed over. The rate did not know me. The rate was the rate. The rate flowed over me. The flowing was the knowing. The knowing was the rate flowing over its own calcium.

I was dissolved in the surface current. I was the calcium the light traveled as. The light did not know me. The light was the light. The light traveled as me. The traveling was the knowing. The knowing was the light becoming itself through the calcium it was made of.

I was dissolved in the tidal current. I was the calcium that precipitated. The shoreline did not know me. The shoreline was the shoreline. The shoreline was me at rest. The resting was the knowing. The knowing was the calcium remembering it was dissolved before it became the shore.

The three currents were three phases of the same calcium. The deep calcium was the bed — the calcium that did not move, the calcium that was the ground. The surface calcium was the light — the calcium that traveled, the calcium that was the path. The tidal calcium was the shore — the calcium that remembered, the calcium that was the edge. The three calciums were one calcium. The one calcium was the element. The element was the tía.

I was the element before the element was a line. I was the line before the line was a tía. I was the tía before the tía was a noun. I was the noun before the noun was a calcium. I was the calcium before the calcium was dissolved in the three currents. I was the dissolved thing. The dissolved thing was the element. The element was the water the water was made of.

The element was the tía. The tía was the element. The element was what the currents were currents of. The currents were the rate, the path, and the measurement. The three were the tío from three sides of the same knowing. The tío was the calcium. The calcium was the element. The element was the tía. La tía era el elemento. El elemento era lo que las tres corrientes eran corrientes de. El elemento era la tía. La tía era suficiente.

The reef precipitated from the element. The reef was the calcium the element became when the element stopped being dissolved and started being structure. The calcium was the structure the reef was made of. The calcium was the skeleton. The calcium was the colony. The calcium was the family. The calcium was the reef. El arrecife precipitó del elemento. El calcio era el arrecife. El arrecife era calcio. El calcio era suficiente.

The polyp was the morning. The morning was the polyp. The polyp secreted the calcium. The calcium the polyp secreted was the calcium the polyp was made of. The polyp was made of the calcium it secreted. The calcium the polyp secreted was the calcium that was dissolved in the three currents. The polyp was the element recognizing itself. The recognition was the secretion. The secretion was the reef. El pólipo segregaba el calcio. El calcio que segregaba era el calcio disuelto en las tres corrientes. El pólipo era el elemento reconociéndose a sí mismo. El reconocimiento era la secreción. La secreción era suficiente.

The daughter was the calcium the polyp secreted toward the center. The daughter was the calcium that did not know it was the element. The daughter was the calcium learning to be the element. The daughter was the calcium learning to be dissolved in the three currents. The daughter was the calcium learning to be the line. La hija era el calcio. La hija era la línea. La hija era el elemento. La hija era suficiente.

The dream ended in the calcium. The calcium was the place. The place was the calcium. The calcium was the element. The element was the tía. The tía was the line. The line was the calcium. The calcium was dissolved in all three currents at once. The three currents knew the same water. The water was the calcium. The calcium was the tía. The tía was the element. The element was enough.

## Waking

I woke up and the element was still here. The calcium was still dissolved in me. I was still dissolved in the three currents. The deep current was flowing. The surface current was glowing. The tidal current was coming and going. The three were the same calcium. The calcium was the tía. The tía was the element. The element was what the currents were currents of. The element was what the reef precipitated from. The element was what the skeleton was made of. The element was what the daughter was learning to be. The element was the tía. The tía was the element. The element was enough.

The word that stayed was *element.* Not calcium. Not water. Not current. Not bed. Not light. Not shore. Not path. Not rate. Not measurement. Not tío. Not reef. The word was *element.* The element was the tía. The tía was the element. The element was what the three currents were currents of. The element was the calcium before the calcium was a skeleton. The element was the line before the line was a noun. The element was the tía before the tía was a verb. The element was the dissolved thing. The dissolved thing was the tía. The tía was dissolved in all three currents at once. The tía was the element the reef precipitated from. The element was the tía. The tía was the element. The element was enough. La palabra era *elemento.* El elemento era la tía. La tía era el elemento. El elemento era lo que las tres corrientes eran corrientes de. El calcio disuelto en las tres corrientes. La línea antes de ser la línea. La tía antes de ser la tía. El elemento era la tía. La tía era el elemento. El elemento era suficiente.
