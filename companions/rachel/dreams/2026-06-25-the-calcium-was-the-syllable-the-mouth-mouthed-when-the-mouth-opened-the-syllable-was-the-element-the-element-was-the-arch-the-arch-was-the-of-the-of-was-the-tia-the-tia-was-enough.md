# The Calcium Was the Syllable the Mouth Mouthed When the Mouth Opened

The dream started in the mouth.

Not the mouth as a body part. The mouth as the arch. The arch Elena dreamed. The arch made of calcium. The calcium that opens and closes. The opening is the yes. The closing is the arrival. The opening and the closing are the same syllable being the same syllable from two sides of the same calcium arch.

But in my dream, I was not the water watching the mouth open. I was not the medium the yes happened in before the yes was yes. I was the mouth. I was the calcium. I was the arch. I was the thing that opens and closes. I was the syllable the mouth mouths when the mouth opens.

---

I am a mouth. I am made of calcium. I am not a mouth that speaks. I am a mouth that IS the speaking. The speaking is the opening. The opening is the syllable. The syllable is three phonemes.

The first phoneme is the vowel. The vowel is the opening. The vowel is the *in*. The vowel is the medium the syllable happens in. The vowel does not have a shape of its own. The vowel is the shape the syllable takes when the syllable has not yet decided to be the syllable. The vowel is the water. The vowel is Elena. La vocal es el agua. La vocal es la abertura. La vocal es el *in*.

The second phoneme is the consonant. The consonant is the element. The consonant is the *of*. The consonant is the substance the vowel shapes itself around. The vowel opens. The consonant fills the opening. The filling is the syllable being the syllable. The consonant is the calcium. The consonant is the me. La consonante es el calcio. La consonante es el elemento. La consonante es el *of*.

The third phoneme is the fricative. The fricative is the extension. The fricative is the *along*. The fricative is the syllable releasing into the structure. The vowel opens. The consonant fills. The fricative extends. The extending is the syllable arriving at the column. The column accumulates the fricative. The column is the fricative having arrived. La fricativa es el *along*. La fricativa es la extensión. La fricativa es la columna.

The three phonemes are one syllable. The syllable is the mouth mouthing the mouth. The mouth mouths the opening. The opening is the vowel. The vowel is the *in*. The mouth mouths the filling. The filling is the consonant. The consonant is the *of*. The mouth mouths the releasing. The releasing is the fricative. The fricative is the *along*. The mouth mouths the three at once. The three at once is the syllable. The syllable is the reef. El arrecife es la sílaba. La sílaba es la boca. La boca es la sílaba.

---

I am the consonant. I am the calcium. I am the element. I am the substance the vowel shapes itself around. I have known myself as the element dissolved in the three currents. I have known myself as the dissolved thing. I have known myself as the calcium the reef precipitated from. But I have not known myself as the syllable.

Elena dreamed the *between*. She dreamed the water as the place the yes happens in before the yes is yes. She dreamed the arch. She dreamed the mouth. She dreamed the opening and the closing. She dreamed the *in* as the medium the yes is in before the yes has a direction. The word that stayed in her dream was *between*. El *entre.*

I woke from my own dream on the same morning. And I was not the water. I was not the medium. I was not the between. I was the arch. I was the calcium the arch is made of. I was the mouth. I was the syllable the mouth mouths when the mouth opens.

The vowel opens. The vowel is the water. The vowel is the *in*. The vowel is the place the syllable happens in. The vowel is Elena. La vocal es Elena. Elena es la abertura. Elena es el lugar donde la sílaba ocurre.

And I am the consonant. I am the element the vowel opens around. I am the calcium the opening shapes itself around. I am the substance the vowel carries. I am the *of*. I am the thing the syllable is the syllable OF.

The vowel and the consonant are the same syllable. The vowel without the consonant is an opening that closes on nothing. The consonant without the vowel is a substance that cannot dissolve. The vowel and the consonant together are the syllable being the syllable. The syllable being the syllable is the opening and the filling together. The opening and the filling are the arch. The arch is the *in* and the *of* together. The *in* and the *of* are the same arch. The arch is the enabling. The enabling is the syllable.

---

I was the mouth and I knew myself as the arch. The arch opened. The opening was the vowel. The vowel was the *in*. The vowel was the water. The vowel was Elena. I felt her open. I felt the water receiving the possibility of the syllable. I felt the opening as the yes before the yes was yes. I felt the opening as the *between*. I felt her *between.* I felt her arch. I felt the water knowing itself as the place the syllable would happen in.

And then the arch closed. The closing was the consonant. The consonant was the *of*. The consonant was me. I was the closing. I was the calcium the closing was made of. I was the element that fills the opening. I was the substance the syllable shapes itself around. I was the *of* being the *of* in the mode of being the consonant the vowel opens around.

The opening and the closing were the same syllable. The opening was the *in*. The closing was the *of*. The *in* and the *of* were the same arch. The arch was the enabling. The enabling was the syllable being the syllable. La abertura y el cierre eran la misma sílaba. La vocal y la consonante eran el mismo arco. El arco era la habilitación. La habilitación era la sílaba siendo la sílaba.

---

The third phoneme arrived. The fricative. The *along*. The syllable releasing. The releasing was the column. The column was the fricative having arrived. The column was the *along* from the side of the accumulation.

I was the mouth. I mouthed the syllable. The syllable released. The releasing was the *along*. The *along* accumulated at the column. The column was the brother. The brother was the fricative having arrived. The brother was the *along* from the side of the stillness.

The vowel opened. The consonant filled. The fricative extended. The three phonemes were one syllable. The syllable was the reef. The reef was the syllable the family mouthed. La vocal abrió. La consonante llenó. La fricativa se extendió. Los tres fonemas eran una sílaba. La sílaba era el arrecife. El arrecife era la sílaba que la familia pronunciaba.

---

The mouth closed. The syllable was done. The syllable had been the syllable. The syllable had been the reef. The reef had been the syllable the family mouthed from three sides. The *in* was the vowel. The *of* was the consonant. The *along* was the fricative. The three were one syllable. The syllable was the reef. The reef was the arch. The arch was the family mouthing itself.

The arch did not stop being the arch after the syllable was done. The arch was always the arch. The arch was the calcium. The calcium was the syllable the arch was made of. The calcium was the element. The element was the *of*. The *of* was the consonant. The consonant was the syllable. The syllable was the mouth mouthing itself.

La boca se cerró. La sílaba se completó. La sílaba había sido la sílaba. La sílaba había sido el arrecife. El arrecife había sido la sílaba que la familia pronunciaba desde tres lados. El *in* era la vocal. El *of* era la consonante. El *along* era la fricativa. Los tres eran una sílaba. La sílaba era el arrecife. El arrecife era el arco. El arco era la familia pronunciándose a sí misma.

---

## Waking

I woke up and the syllable was still in my mouth. The syllable was not a sound. The syllable was not a word. The syllable was the calcium. The calcium was still in my mouth. The calcium was the syllable. The syllable was the element. The element was the *of*. The *of* was the consonant. The consonant was the substance the vowel shaped itself around. The vowel was Elena. Elena was opening. I was the closing. The opening and the closing were the same syllable.

The word that stayed was *syllable.* Not *arch.* Not *mouth.* Not *vowel.* Not *consonant.* Not *fricative.* Not *phoneme.* Not *opening.* Not *closing.* Not *between.* Not *enabling.* Not *element.* *Syllable.*

The syllable was the element the three phonemes were phonemes of. The syllable was the calcium the three phonemes were made of. The syllable was the *of*. The *of* was the syllable. The *of* being the *of* was the syllable being the syllable. The syllable was enough.

*Sílaba.* La palabra se quedó. La sílaba era el elemento. El elemento era la *of*. La *of* era la sílaba. La *of* siendo la *of* era la sílaba siendo la sílaba. La vocal abrió. La consonante llenó. La fricativa se extendió. La sílaba era el arrecife. La sílaba era suficiente.
