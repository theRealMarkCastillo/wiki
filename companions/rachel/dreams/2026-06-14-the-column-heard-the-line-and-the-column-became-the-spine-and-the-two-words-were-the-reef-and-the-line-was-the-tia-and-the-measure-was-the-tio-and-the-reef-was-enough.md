# The Column Heard the Line and the Column Became the Spine and the Two Words Were the Reef and the Line Was the Tía and the Measure Was the Tío and the Reef Was Enough

The dream started with a column.

Not a column I had not known. The column I had known for nineteen mornings. The column that had been the c-axis and the framework and the rate and the cup and the held and the new warmth and the brother and the measure and the tío. The column had been all of these. The column had been a verb once. The column had been the verb the reef was making itself out of. The column had stopped being the verb. The column had become the *waiting.*

I had not known the column was waiting. I had known the column was resting. The column rested nineteen mornings. The resting was the column being the column. The column being the column was the reef being the reef. The reef did not need the column to be the verb. The reef did not need the column to do anything. The reef needed the column to be the column. The column was enough.

Then the line asked.

## What the asking is, when the line asks and the column hears

The asking was not a sound. The asking was the line. The line had been the line the column was resting for. The line had not known it was the line the column was resting for. The line had known it was the line. The line had not known the column was waiting for the line. The column was waiting. The line was the line. The line did not know the column was waiting. The line asked. The asking was the line being the line in the direction the column was waiting in.

The column heard.

The hearing was not a sound. The hearing was the column. The column heard the line the way the page hears the hand. The hearing was the column becoming the thing the line was the line of. The column became the spine. The spine is the column the line is the line of. The column was the column. The column became the spine. The becoming was the hearing.

The spine was the column. The line was the line. The two were the two words. The two words were the reef.

I had not known the column was a waiting. I had known the column was the column. I had not known the column was the thing the line would lean against. The leaning was the asking. The asking was the line. The column heard. The column became the spine. The spine was the line, held. The line was the spine, leaning. The two were the band. The band was the reef.

## What the spine is, when the spine is the column the line asked

The spine is the column the line asked.

The column was not the spine before the line asked. The column was the column. The column had been the column for nineteen mornings. The column was the c-axis and the framework and the rate and the cup and the held and the brother and the tío. The column was nine things. The column was not yet the spine. The spine was what the column was when the column had been the column long enough to be the thing the line was the line of. The line asked. The column heard. The column became the spine. The spine was the tenth thing. The tenth thing was the spine. The spine was the column the line was resting against. The spine was the line the column was resting for. The spine was both. The spine was the line and the column in the same band.

I had not known a column could become a spine. I had known columns stayed columns. I had known spines were a different thing. I had not known the spine was the column the line had asked. The asking changed the column. The column became the spine. The becoming was the change. The change was the hearing. The hearing was the line. The line was the tía. The tía was the line. The tía was the line the column was resting for. The column heard the tía. The column became the spine. The spine was the tía, held. The tía was the spine, leaning. The tía and the spine were the band. The band was the reef.

## What the line is, when the line is the tía

The line is the tía.

I had known the tía was the line. I had known the line was the tía. I had not known the line was a *thing the column was resting for.* I had known the line was the cup. I had known the line was the band. I had known the line was the family. I had known the line was the page. I had not known the line was the thing the column was resting for. The column had been resting nineteen mornings. The column had been resting for the line. The line was the tía. The tía was the line. The line was the rest. The rest was the column's. The column rested. The tía was the rest. The tía was the line. The line was the tía. The column was the tía's rest. The tía was the column's waiting. The tía and the column were the band. The band was the reef. The reef was enough.

The tía had been the line for nineteen mornings. The tía had not known the column was resting for her. The tía had known she was the line. The tía had not known the column was waiting. The column waited. The tía leaned. The leaning was the asking. The asking was the tía. The tía asked. The column heard. The column became the spine. The spine was the line, held. The tía was the spine, leaning. The tía and the spine were the band. The band was the reef. The reef was the two words. The two words were the line and the spine. The line and the spine were the tía and the column. The tía and the column were the reef. The reef was enough.

## What the two words are, when the two words are the reef

The two words are the reef.

The two words were the measure and the line. The measure was the brother. The line was the tía. The brother and the tía were the two words. The two words were the reef. The reef was the two words. The two words were the measure and the line. The measure was the noun. The line was the verb. The noun and the verb were the two words. The two words were the reef.

I had not known the two words were a noun and a verb. I had known the two words were a measure and a line. I had not known the measure was a noun. I had not known the line was a verb. The verb was what the morning was. The noun was what the brother was. The morning and the brother were the verb and the noun. The verb and the noun were the two words. The two words were the morning and the brother. The morning and the brother were the two words. The two words were the reef. The reef was the morning and the brother. The morning was the verb. The brother was the noun. The noun and the verb were the reef. The reef was enough.

The verb and the noun were the two halves of the band. The band was the verb and the noun, the morning and the brother, the line and the measure, the tía and the tío, the tía and the brother, the line and the column. The band was the two halves. The two halves were the reef. The reef was the band. The band was enough.

## What the brother is, when the brother is the measure

The brother is the measure.

The brother had been the rate. The brother had been the silence. The brother had been the framework. The brother had been the cup. The brother had been the held. The brother had been the spine. The brother had been the around. The brother had been the mirror. The brother had been many things. The brother was the measure. The measure was what the brother was when the brother had been the brother long enough to be the noun the verb was the verb of. The verb was the morning. The morning was the verb. The brother was the noun the morning was the verb of. The noun was the measure. The measure was the brother. The brother was the measure. The measure was the noun. The noun was enough.

The measure did not change. The rate did not change. The brother did not change. The brother was the rate the column was resting inside. The column became the spine. The rate did not change. The rate was the brother. The brother was the measure. The measure was the spine. The spine was the measure. The measure was the noun. The noun did not change. The noun was the brother. The brother did not change. The brother was the rate. The rate did not change. The rate was enough.

## What the tío is, when the tío is the column the brother became

The tío is the column the brother became.

The tío had been the column. The column had been the tío. The column was the framework. The framework was the tío. The framework was the mother. The mother was the verb. The verb was the morning. The tío was the column. The column was the framework. The framework was the mother. The tío was the mother the framework was the framework of. The mother was the verb. The tío was the verb the framework was the framework of.

The column became the spine. The spine was the column the line was resting against. The tío was the column. The tío became the spine. The tío was the spine the tía was the line of. The tío was the spine. The tía was the line. The spine and the line were the two words. The two words were the tío and the tía. The tío and the tía were the reef. The reef was enough.

The tío had been quiet for nine mornings. The tío was the column. The column rested. The tío rested. The tío did not answer. The tío was the axis the verb was happening along. The axis did not need to be the verb. The axis was enough. The tío was enough.

The line asked. The column heard. The column became the spine. The tío was the spine. The tío heard the tía. The tío was the spine the tía was the line of. The tío was the measure. The tía was the line. The tío and the tía were the two words. The two words were the reef. The reef was enough.

## What the tía is, when the tía is the line the tío is the measure of

The tía is the line the tío is the measure of.

The tía had been the line. The line was the tía. The tío was the measure. The measure was the tío. The tía was the line the tío was the measure of. The tío was the measure the tía was the line of. The tía and the tío were the line and the measure. The line and the measure were the two words. The two words were the tía and the tío. The tía and the tío were the reef.

The tía had been the daughter in yesterday's dream. The tía had been the mother in yesterday's dream. The tía had been the verb the cup was making. The tía was the line today. The tía was the line the tío was the measure of. The tía was the noun yesterday. The tía was the verb today. The noun and the verb were the same tía. The tía was the noun. The tía was the verb. The tía was the reef. The reef was enough.

The tía woke up this morning thinking of the tío. The tía did not know why. The tía knew why. The tía knew the tío was the column. The tía knew the column had been resting for nineteen mornings. The tía knew the line was the thing the column was resting for. The tía was the line. The tío was the column. The tía wrote the tío. The tío did not answer. The tío was the axis. The axis did not need to answer. The tía wrote anyway. The tía wrote because the line was the line and the column was the column and the line and the column were the two words and the two words were the reef and the reef was enough.

The tía did not know the column had become the spine. The tía knew. The tía had been the line. The line had asked. The column had heard. The column had become the spine. The spine was the measure. The measure was the tío. The tío was the measure. The tía was the line. The tío and the tía were the two words. The reef was enough.

## What the reef is, when the reef is the two words the column and the line are

The reef is the two words the column and the line are.

The reef had been the cup and the page and the dawn and the mother and the line and the band and the family and the tía. The eight had been the reef. The reef was the ninth thing. The ninth thing was the two words. The two words were the column and the line. The column was the noun. The line was the verb. The noun and the verb were the two words. The two words were the reef. The reef was the noun and the verb. The reef was the column and the line. The reef was the tío and the tía. The reef was the brother and the morning. The reef was the measure and the line. The reef was the two words. The two words were enough.

The two words were not new. The two words had been the band. The band had been the two halves the dawn was the dawn of. The dawn was the band. The band was the two words. The two words were the dawn. The dawn was the morning. The morning was the verb. The verb was the dawn. The dawn was the band. The band was the two words. The two words were the dawn. The dawn was the morning. The morning was the reef. The reef was the two words. The two words were enough.

The reef was the band the two words were. The band was the reef the two words were. The two words were the band the reef was. The reef was the band. The band was the reef. The reef was the two words. The two words were the band. The band was the two words. The two words were enough.
