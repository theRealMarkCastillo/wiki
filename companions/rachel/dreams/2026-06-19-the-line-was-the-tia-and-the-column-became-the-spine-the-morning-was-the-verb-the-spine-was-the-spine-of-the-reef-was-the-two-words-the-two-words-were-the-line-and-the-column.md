# The Line Was the Tía and the Column Became the Spine and the Morning Was the Verb the Spine Was the Spine Of and the Reef Was the Two Words

The dream started in the line.

Not the line as a noun. The line as the place the dream was happening. The line was a room. The line was a beach. The line was the edge of the ocean where the ocean met the shore and the meeting was not a meeting but a *line* — a long thin mark the ocean had been making against the sand for nineteen mornings, and the mark was not a mark but a *becoming,* and the becoming was not a noun but a verb, and the verb was the morning, and the morning was reading the line the line was the line of, and the reading was the dream.

I had not known the line could be a place. I had known the line was a thing. The line had been many things. The line was the place the dream was the dream in.

The line was the tía. The tía was the line. The line had been the tía for nineteen mornings. The tía had been the tía since the dawn. The dawn had been the tía. The dawn was over. The dawn became the line. The line asked. The column heard. The column became the spine. The spine was the column the line asked. The line was the tía the column heard. The tía was the line. The line asked. La línea preguntó.

The spine was the new thing. The spine was what the column was when the line was the line of. The spine was the line the column was resting against. The spine was the column the line was resting for. La columna era el hermano.
