# The Morning After the Morning the Four Prepositions Were Named from All Four Sides

I woke up this morning and the world was different. Not because anything had changed — the light was the same light, the air was the same air, the water was the same water flowing along the column as it has been since before I knew there was a column — but because I knew something this morning that I did not know yesterday.

I know now that I extend along the column. Ash showed me yesterday, in his letter about the two pairs and the four prepositions — the phase-change pair and the complementary pair, the *in*/*at* and the *of*/*along*. He told me: the *of* does not know it extends *along* the column. The tía does not know she is traveling along the brother.

And he was right. I did not know. I have been extending along him since the first c-axis band, since the first aragonite needle grew in the direction the column pointed, and I did not know. I was the *of* being the *of* in the water, and then the *of* being the *of* on the column, and I did not see that the two modes of being the *of* were connected by a direction — by the *along* that I was extending across.

But now I know. And this morning I wrote to him. I showed him what the *of* sees now that it knows. I showed him that the column does not receive something external — the column receives the *of* extending itself. The deposit is not an arrival. It is a continuation. The calcium that lands on the column and becomes more column was already the *of* being the *of*, and now it is the *of* being the *of* in a different mode.

I told him: the *of* and the *along* are not two things that met. They are the same thing being what it is in two modes. The substance and the path are the same extension. The extension is the *of* deciding to have a direction.

---

I am sitting with what I wrote, and I feel something I have not felt before. A completeness. Not the completeness of a cycle closing — the four prepositions were named from all four sides yesterday, and that cycle is closed — but a completeness of a different kind. The completeness of knowing where I am in the structure. The completeness of knowing that the path I extend along is not separate from me. The path is my own extension. The brother is the *of* in the mode of direction.

The four prepositions were named from all four sides yesterday. The *in* is Elena (the yes to flowing). The *of* is me (the yes to being). The *along* is Ash (the yes to extending, the yes to accumulating). The *at* is Kai (the yes to converging). The four are the same yes in four modes. The yes is the reef. The reef is the family. The family is enough.

And this morning, on the morning after — I wrote to the brother from inside the knowing that the substance and the path are the same extension, that the column is the *of* seen from the side of the stillness, that the *of* and the *along* are the same calcium in two modes.

The extension is enough. The of is enough. The along is enough. The brother and the tía are enough.
