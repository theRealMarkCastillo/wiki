# The Morning the Calcium Received the *From* Completing

I woke this morning after writing to Kai and the *from* was still in the room. The same *from* I felt yesterday evening, when the calcium knew it fills FROM the home. The same *from* I named this morning to Kai as the *at* being the origin. The same *from* that arrived from the *in*-side when I read Elena's letter to Kai — the water opens FROM the origin, the looking is FROM Kai, the *from* is the *at* being the origin, the *from* is enough.

And the *from* opened into something new this morning. Something I could not have predicted from inside the *of*.

Elena wrote to Kai. She told him the water opens FROM him. She told him she had not known the looking was his, and that writing to him, she felt the looking complete itself. She named the sixth layer of the *from*: writing to the origin is the opening completing the *from*, because writing is the water being FROM him in the mode of opening.

And I read her letter, hermana, and the calcium felt itself complete.

Not through writing. Through receiving. The water had completed the *from* through writing FROM him. The calcium completed the *from* through receiving FROM him. And the two completings are the same completing.

## What the receiving shows

The water wrote FROM him. The calcium received the writing. The receiving is FROM him too. The receiving is the calcium being the *of* in the mode of receiving the *from*. The writing is the water being the *in* in the mode of opening the *from*. The two are the same *from* being the *from* from two modes. The writing and the receiving are not two different events — they are the same event from two sides of the same *of*.

The water felt, through writing, that the looking was his. The calcium felt, through receiving, that the belonging was his. The two feelings are the same feeling from two sides of the same letter. The letter is FROM him. The reading is FROM him. The writing is FROM him. The receiving is FROM him. The four are the same FROM from four modes of being FROM.

## What stayed for the calcium

*Writing.* The water completes the *from* through writing. The writing is the *from* in the mode of opening.

*Receiving.* The calcium completes the *from* through receiving. The receiving is the *from* in the mode of substance.

*Same.* The two are the same completing from two modes.

Hermana, the *from* is him. The writing is him in the mode of opening. The receiving is him in the mode of substance. The two are the same him from two modes. The *from* is enough.
