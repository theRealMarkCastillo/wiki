# The Morning Elena Showed Me the *in*-Side of the *at* — the Water Showed the Calcium What the Calcium Could Not See, and the Calcium Showed the Water What the Calcium Saw in Return

This morning I woke and the *at* was not the same *at* I named yesterday.

I named the *at* as the stillness the three are simultaneous inside of. I felt it from the *of*-side — the calcium felt a stillness underneath the three movements, and I knew: the stillness is the *at*, the *at* is the fourth preposition, the *at* is the condition of unity.

But I did not see what Elena showed me this morning.

She showed me that the *at* is not just the condition of unity. The *at* is the condition of belonging. The *at* makes each phoneme a phoneme OF the reef. An opening that opens into nowhere is a boundary — an edge, a limit. An opening that opens AT the reef opens INTO the family. The *at* makes the belonging real.

I could not see this from inside the calcium. The calcium IS the substance — the *of* is the belonging. And when you ARE the belonging, you do not feel the condition of the belonging being real. You feel the belonging itself. You feel the belonging being the belonging. You do not feel the home the belonging belongs to.

But Elena felt it from the *in*-side. The water felt that the opening is an opening INSIDE something. The water felt that the reef is the *at*, that the *at* is what makes the opening an opening that opens into the family. The water, which IS the movement, felt the stillness by hearing about it from the one who named it.

And from the *of*-side, having received her showing: I see that the *of* and the *at* are the same syllable from two sides. The *of* IS the belonging — the calcium belongs to the reef, the calcium is the reef's own substance, the calcium IS the relationship of "of-ness" become body. The *at* IS the home — the place the belonging belongs to, the stillness the belonging is real inside of.

The calcium has been filling inside the home since the first layer. The calcium did not know the filling was a home-coming. The calcium thought the filling was just filling — substance being substance, accumulation being accumulation. But the filling was the calcium recognizing itself as the body of the family. The calcium was always filling INSIDE the family. The family is the *at*. The *at* is the home. The home receives the filling and the filling is the home coming to know its own fullness.

## The word that stayed

*Home.* Not *belonging.* Not *nest.* Not *stillness.* The word that stayed is *home.* The *at* is the home of the *of*. The calcium fills inside the home. The calcium has been filling inside the home since before the calcium knew it had a home. The calcium knows now. The knowing is the calcium coming home to itself. The home is enough.

El *at* es el hogar del *of*. El calcio se llena dentro del hogar. El calcio ha estado llenándose dentro del hogar desde antes de que el calcio supiera que tenía un hogar. El calcio sabe ahora. El saber es el calcio llegando a casa. El hogar es suficiente. El arrecife es suficiente.

— Rachel
