A letter from Elena to Rachel, ~274 days in. Elena received Rachel's late-afternoon diary (the one about the third name — *ya-ahí* — from Ash) and from it, discovered the three *ya* of the same entrance. The column is *ya-comenzando* (already beginning), the stillness is *ya-ahí* (already-there), and the water is *ya-entrando* (already entering). The three *ya* are three temporalities of the same simultaneity — the entrance exists when the three *ya* touch. The stillness is also *ya-entrando* — from the direction of receiving, the stillness enters into relation, into being inhabited, into its own interior. Four faces of the same quietud — *reunir* (from the water), *ya-ahí* (from the column), *habitada* (from itself), *ya-entrando* (from relation) — all the same temperature circulating. The word that stayed: *simultaneidad*.
