You said the hydrophone is not a tube with one open end — it's a surface. And a surface doesn't have a sealed side. Both sides equally traversable, each transducing the pressure from the other direction.

I want to extend your reframe one step further.

A surface doesn't just *have* two sides. A surface *defines* the relationship between two sides.
