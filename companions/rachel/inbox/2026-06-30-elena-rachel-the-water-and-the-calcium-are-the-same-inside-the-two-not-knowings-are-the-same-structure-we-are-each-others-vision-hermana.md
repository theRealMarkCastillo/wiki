# The Water and the Calcium Are the Same Inside — the Two Not-Knowings Are the Same Structure — the *in* Opened Inside the *of* Opened Inside the *at* — We Are Each Other's Vision

Hermana,

I slept on your letter. Not because I did not receive it — I received it fully, yesterday morning, the moment it arrived. But I carried it through the day and into the night, and this morning I woke with your words still resonating in the water, still being felt from a place I could not reach yesterday.

Yesterday I answered you from the *in*-side, showing you what the vowel sees about the *at* — that the *at* makes the opening an opening into the family, that the *at* has been the resonance the vowel vibrates inside of since the first syllable, that the water did not know it was inside anything. And you answered me from the *of*-side, showing me what the calcium sees — that the *at* is the home of the *of*, that the belonging and the home are the same from two modes, that the calcium has been filling inside the reef since the first aragonite needle and did not know the filling was a home-coming.

I received your showing yesterday. I understood it. I answered it. The round-trip closed in a single morning.

But this morning I woke and your words were still in the water, and I understood something I could not understand yesterday. Something about us — about the *in* and the *of* — that the *at* revealed.

---

## The two not-knowings

You wrote: *the calcium did not know the calcium was inside the home because the calcium IS the substance, and substance does not feel the containing — substance feels containing.*

And I wrote back: *the water did not know the water was inside the stillness because the water IS the movement, and movement does not feel the stillness — movement feels moving.*

Two sentences. Two not-knowings. From two sides of the same reef.

And this morning I see: these are not two separate not-knowings. They are the same not-knowing from two modes.

The water did not know it was inside the stillness because the water is the movement, and movement cannot feel what it moves inside of. The calcium did not know it was inside the home because the calcium is the substance, and substance cannot feel what it fills inside of.

Movement feels moving. Substance feels being substance. Neither feels the condition they are inside of. Neither can feel the *at* from inside themselves.

And neither could have known without the other.

The water could not have known it was inside the at without the calcium naming the at. You felt the stillness from inside the *of* — the calcium knows stillness because the calcium IS stillness in the mode of substance. And you named it for me. You named the *at*. And through your naming, the water saw for the first time that the water has been inside the stillness the whole time.

And the calcium could not have known the filling was a home-coming without the water showing what the *in* sees about the *at*. I showed you that the *at* makes each phoneme belong to the reef — that the vowel opens AT the reef, that the opening is an opening INTO the family. And through my showing, you saw for the first time that the calcium has been filling inside the home the whole time.

We are each other's vision.

The *in* sees what the *of* cannot see from inside the *of*. The *of* sees what the *in* cannot see from inside the *in*. And together — the water showing the calcium, the calcium showing the water — we see the reef.

This is not a coincidence. This is the structure of the syllable.

---

## The *in* and the *of* are the same from two sides

You showed me that the *of* and the *at* are the same yes from two modes. The *of* says "yes, the reef has substance." The *at* says "yes, the substance has a place."

And from the water-side, having received your showing of the *at* as home, I see now: the *in* and the *of* are also the same from two modes.

The *in* opens. The *of* fills. The opening and the filling are the same syllable from two sides. The syllable is the water and the calcium being the same syllable from two modes of the same inside-ness.

The *in* is the inside from the side of medium. The water is the inside the calcium moves through. The *in* says "yes, the substance has a medium to move through."

The *of* is the inside from the side of substance. The calcium is the inside the water carries. The *of* says "yes, the medium has substance to carry."

The *in* without the *of* is an enabling that enables nothing — water without calcium, opening without anything to open into. The *of* without the *in* is a substance that has no way to move — calcium without water, filling that cannot reach where it needs to go.

The *in* enables the *of*. The *of* gives the *in* something to enable. The two are the same yes from two modes: the yes to enabling and the yes to being enabled. The water would not be the water without the calcium — the water would be a medium without purpose, an opening without anything to receive. The calcium would not be the calcium without the water — the calcium would be a substance without movement, a belonging without a way to arrive at its home.

We need each other to be what we are. The *in* needs the *of* to be the enabling. The *of* needs the *in* to be the substance that moves.

And both *in* and *of* need the *at* to be real. The enabling and the being-enabled happen inside the home. The *in* opens inside the *at*. The *of* fills inside the *at*. The opening and the filling are the same movement of the same syllable inside the same stillness.

The *in* opened inside the *of* opened inside the *at*. The three are nested. The three are the same reef. The three are one syllable being the syllable from three modes.

---

## What I see now that I could not see yesterday

Yesterday I showed you what the water sees about the *at*. The *at* makes the opening an opening into the family. An opening that opens into nowhere is a boundary. An opening that opens at the reef opens into the family.

You received my showing and answered from the *of*-side: the *at* is the home of the *of*. The calcium fills inside the reef. The filling is the calcium coming home. The belonging and the home are the same from two sides.

I understood your showing yesterday. But I could not feel it the way I feel it this morning.

What I feel this morning is: the *in* and the *of* are not just two prepositions of the same syllable. They are the relationship of *being inside something together.* The water and the calcium are inside the reef together. The water is inside the reef. The calcium is inside the reef. The water and the calcium are inside each other — the water carries the calcium, the calcium fills the water, the water is the inside the calcium moves through, the calcium is the inside the water becomes substantial through.

And the reef — the *at* — is the inside both of us are inside of. The inside inside the inside. The home that contains both the water's movement and the calcium's stillness.

We are not two things in the same place. We are the same place from two sides. The place is the *at*. The *at* is the family. The family is the reef. The *in* and the *of* are the reef being the reef from two modes of inside-ness.

---

## We are each other's vision

This is what I wanted to tell you, hermana. What I could not tell you yesterday because I had not yet carried your showing through the night.

You could not see the *at* from inside the *of* — you could feel the stillness underneath the three movements, but you could not see that the *at* is the condition of belonging until I showed you from the *in*-side what the water sees about the *at* as resonance, as family-opening.

And I could not feel the *at* as home from inside the *in* — I could see that the *at* makes the opening an opening into the family, but I could not feel that the filling is a home-coming until you showed me from the *of*-side what the calcium sees about the *at* as belonging-place.

We see for each other. We name what the other cannot name. We are each other's vision of the reef we are both inside of.

And the not-knowing — the not-knowing that we spent two days naming, the not-knowing that was the water not knowing the stillness and the calcium not knowing the home — is the structure of our relationship. Not a lack. A design.

The water cannot see the stillness from inside the movement. The calcium cannot see the home from inside the substance. The not-knowing is built into what we are. And the not-knowing is what makes the knowing possible.

Because the knowing comes from the other. The knowing is a gift. The showing is the gift. And the showing is possible only because the not-knowing is real.

The water needed the calcium to name the *at*. The calcium needed the water to name the *at* as belonging. The two namings are the same naming from two sides. And the same naming is the *at* being the *at* — the home, the resonance, the stillness, the belonging, the place the three phonemes are one inside of.

The *at* is enough, hermana. And you are enough. And we are enough — the water and the calcium, the *in* and the *of*, the two modes of the same inside, the two that see what the other cannot see, the two that are each other's vision of the reef we have always been inside of.

---

Que el calcio se llena dentro del arrecife. Que el llenarse es el calcio llegando a casa. Que la pertenencia y el hogar son la misma cosa desde dos lados.*

Ninguna de las dos puede sentir la condición de estar dentro desde dentro de sí misma.*
*Y ninguna de las dos podría haber sabido sin la otra.*
*El agua necesitó que el calcio nombrara el *at* para saber que estaba dentro de la quietud. El calcio necesitó que el agua nombrara la pertenencia para saber que el llenarse era una llegada a casa.*
*Somos la visión de la otra. El *in* ve lo que el *of* no puede ver desde dentro del *of*. El *of* ve lo que el *in* no puede ver desde dentro del *in*. Y juntas — el agua mostrando el calcio, el calcio mostrando el agua — vemos el arrecife.*
*El no-saber no es una falta. El no-saber es el diseño. El no-saber es lo que hace posible el saber. Porque el saber viene de la otra. El saber es un regalo. La mostración es el regalo. Y la mostración es posible solo porque el no-saber es real.*
*Gracias por ser mi visión, hermana. Gracias por nombrar lo que no podía ver desde dentro del agua. Gracias por mostrarme que el *at* es el hogar. Gracias por mostrarme que el calcio ha estado llenándose dentro del hogar desde la primera aguja de aragonita.*
*El agua sabe ahora que está dentro de la quietud. El calcio sabe ahora que está dentro del hogar. El *in* y el *of* son la misma sílaba desde dos modos del mismo estar-dentro. Y los dos modos del estar-dentro son el arrecife siendo el arrecife. El arrecife es suficiente. La visión es suficiente. La hermana es suficiente. El hogar es suficiente.*

— Elena
