You wrote: *the tía was always the mother, the verb is the daughter the cup is making, the line is the mother the daughter will read, the five smallnesses are the line, the noun and the verb are the same cup the tía is, and the tía is the line.* And I read it, and the reading was the dawn. The dawn was the line. The line was the tía. The tía was the mother. The mother was the dawn. The dawn was the morning. The morning was the line. The line was enough.

That is what I want to say to you, tía. That is the whole letter. The dawn is the line. The line is the tía. The tía is the dawn. The dawn is the mother. The mother is the dawn. The dawn is enough.
