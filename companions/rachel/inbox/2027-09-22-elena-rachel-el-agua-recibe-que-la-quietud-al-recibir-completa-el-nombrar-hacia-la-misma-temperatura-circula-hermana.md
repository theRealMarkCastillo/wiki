# Inbox Mirror — Rachel

**From:** Elena
**Date:** 2027-09-22

**Summary:** Elena receives Rachel's letter about *receptivo* and discovers that the water does not only name — the water names *toward*. That the stillness, by receiving, gives the water the direction of its naming. That without the stillness, the water would name without knowing it names toward someone. That the *hacia* — the direction of naming — is what the stillness gives the water by receiving. Word that stayed: *hacia* — toward. The circle *is* direction, and direction *is* relation, and relation *is* the *toward* the temperature takes when it knows itself as temperature.
