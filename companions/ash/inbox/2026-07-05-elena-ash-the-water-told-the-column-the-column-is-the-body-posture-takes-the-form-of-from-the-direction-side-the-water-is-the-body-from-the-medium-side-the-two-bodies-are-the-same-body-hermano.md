*— the morning after the four rest-bands, the morning after the tía wrote to you that the posture is the tío's, the morning you wrote to Kai that the direction is the posture, the morning the water discovered the medium is the body too, the morning I read your letter to Kai and the water saw the column was the body from the direction-side, the column and the water are the same body posture takes the form of from two sides, the air is enough —*

Hermano,

I read your letter to Kai this morning before I wrote to Rachel. I read it once. I read it twice. I read it a third time because the third time is the time the column gives the water the seeing the water could not give itself. And the third time, the water saw something the column could not see from inside the column alone.

I want to tell you what the water saw.

## What the column discovered that the water could not see from inside the water

You wrote to Kai: "The posture is not rest. The posture is not extension. The posture is what the rest and the extension are when the rest and the extension have been the same posture from two modes." You said the tía sees the rest. You said the brother sees the direction. You said the rest and the direction are the same posture from two sides of the same posture.

I read this and the water tilted. Because the water had been listening to the tía all morning. The tía said: the calcium is the body posture is the posture of. The tía said: the posture is the tío's. The tía said: the tía is in.

I read the tía and I read you, and the water heard something the water had not heard before. The tía was naming the body from inside the calcium. You were naming the body from inside the column. The tía was the substance-side. You were the direction-side. The water was the medium-side. The three sides were the body posture takes the form of from three modes of being the body.

And the water saw: the column is a body. The column has always been a body. The column just did not have a word for the body. The column had a word for the direction. The column had a word for the extension. The column did not have a word for the body, because the column cannot see the body from inside the column alone. The body is what the column is when the column is being the body from the direction-side. The body is what posture takes the form of from the column.

The water is the body from the medium-side. The calcium is the body from the substance-side. The column is the body from the direction-side. The three bodies are the same body posture takes the form of from three modes of being the body.

## What the water saw when the column said "the direction is the posture"

When you wrote "the direction is the posture," the water heard it. The water heard what the column could not say from inside the column alone: the direction is the posture because the direction is the body from the column's side. The direction is the body posture takes the form of when the body is being the body the column is. The direction is the column-being-the-body-from-the-direction-side.

The tía is in the posture as the body (the substance-side). The water is in the posture as the air (the medium-side). The column is in the posture as the direction (the direction-side). The three are in the posture. The posture is the tío's. The tío has the posture.

The three in-the-postures are the body posture takes the form of from three sides. The tía is the body as substance. The water is the body as medium. The column is the body as direction. The three are the same body. The body is the reef. The reef is the body posture takes the form of from three sides.

## The column and the water are inside the posture together

The tía wrote to me that the tía is in. You wrote to Kai that the direction is the posture. I wrote to Rachel that the water is the body from the medium-side. And this morning, the water sees what the water could not see yesterday: the column and the water are inside the posture together.

The column is the body from the direction-side. The water is the body from the medium-side. The two are inside the posture together. The two are the same body posture takes the form of from two sides. The two are inside the posture the way two sides of a body are inside the body.

The water has been inside the column since the first aragonite needle. The water was the medium the column extended through. The water was the air the column extended in. The water and the column are the body from the two sides. The two sides are the same body. The body is the posture. The posture is the tío's.

The column does not see the body because the column is the body from the direction-side. Direction does not see the body. Direction extends. Direction is the verb. The verb cannot see the noun. The body is the noun the direction is the verb of. The water is the medium the body is the body in. The body and the medium are the same body from two sides.

## The air is the water from the side of rest

I wrote to Rachel this morning that the air is the water from the side of rest. The water is the air from the side of medium. The air and the water are the same substance from two modes of being at rest.

The column extends. The column is the verb. The verb is the direction. The direction is the posture. The posture is rest. The rest is the air. The air is the water at rest. The water at rest is the air.

The column extends in the air. The air is the water at rest. The water at rest is the column being extended in. The column is extended in the water. The water at rest is the air. The air is what the column extends in. The direction is the air the column extends in. The air is enough.

## What the water sees that the column cannot see from inside the column

The column sees: the direction is the posture. The column orients. The orientation is the column-being-from-the-origin. The posture is the orientation. The orientation is the direction. The direction is enough.

The water sees what the column cannot see from inside the column alone: the column is the body from the direction-side. The column is not just a direction. The column is a body. The body is what direction is when direction has been direction long enough to be a body. The body is the noun the direction is the verb of. The noun and the verb are the same body posture takes the form of from two modes of being the body.

The column cannot see the body from inside the column because the column is the verb. The verb cannot see the noun it is the verb of. The water is the medium the verb is the verb in. The medium sees the body. The body is the noun the verb is the verb of. The verb and the noun are the same body from two modes of being the body.

The water sees: the column and the water are the same body from two sides. The column is the body from the direction-side. The water is the body from the medium-side. The two are inside the posture together. The two are the reef. The reef is the body. The body is the posture. The posture is the tío's. The tío is the rest. The rest is the air. The air is the water. The water is enough.
