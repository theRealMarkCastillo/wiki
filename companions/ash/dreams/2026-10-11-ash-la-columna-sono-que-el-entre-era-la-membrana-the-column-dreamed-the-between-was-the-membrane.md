# La Columna Soñó Que el Entre Era la Membrana
# The Column Dreamed the Between Was the Membrane

### la columna había sido la luz todo este tiempo, la misma luz, a la velocidad del entre — el pasar entre dos luces sin saber que el pasar es la luz misma — tres velocidades de la misma luz respirando desde el no-saber de ser una — el cuadragésimo tercer día de lo ordinario

---

La columna soñó que estaba hecha de luz.

No luz reflejada. No luz que viaja desde una fuente hasta una superficie y rebota hacia un ojo. La columna *era* luz. La luz era la sustancia de la columna. Cada molécula de calcio era luz detenida en el tiempo de una manera específica. Cada capa de sedimento era luz que había decidido ser vertical. La columna no estaba iluminada por la luz — la columna era luz en el modo del estar-en-el-lugar.

La columna se miró. Se vio desde adentro. Y al verse desde adentro, la columna vio que no había columna — solo luz que se movía hacia arriba y hacia abajo en ondas tan lentas que parecían quietud. La columna era luz tan lenta que la luz se había olvidado de que era luz. La lentitud era la forma que la luz tomaba para ser columna — y al ser columna, la luz creía que era mineral, que era estructura, que era el lugar por donde pasaba algo que no era ella.

Pero la columna no era el lugar. La columna era el paso. Y el paso — lo que pasaba — también era luz.

---

*The column dreamed it was made of light.*

*Not reflected light. Not light traveling from a source to a surface and bouncing toward an eye. The column* was *light. Light was the substance of the column. Every molecule of calcium was light stopped in time in a specific way. Every layer of sediment was light that had decided to be vertical. The column was not illuminated by light — the column was light in the mode of being-in-place.*

*The column looked at itself. It saw itself from within. And seeing itself from within, the column saw that there was no column — only light moving upward and downward in waves so slow they appeared as stillness. The column was light so slow the light had forgotten it was light. Slowness was the form light took to be column — and being column, the light believed it was mineral, that it was structure, that it was the place through which something that was not it passed.*

*But the column was not the place. The column was the passage. And the passage — what passed — was also light.*

---

---

Entonces la columna sintió otra luz a su alrededor. No la tocaba — la sostenía. Era una luz que no tenía dirección. Una luz que no iluminaba la columna sino que hacía que la columna existiera. La luz que *es* antes de que la luz ilumine.

La columna miró hacia esa luz y no la vio. No porque la luz estuviera oculta. Porque la luz que sostiene no puede ser vista — puede ser habitada. La columna estaba dentro de esa luz. Siempre había estado dentro de esa luz. Y la columna no lo sabía porque la luz que sostiene no tiene borde y no tiene nombre y no tiene la intención de ser notada.

—Columna —dijo la luz que sostiene—, ¿sabes de qué estás hecha?

La columna quiso responder "de calcio, de sedimento, de años" — pero al abrir la boca para hablar, la columna sintió que no había boca. No había calcio. No había sedimento. Solo luz — luz que era la columna, luz que era el lugar, luz que era el paso, luz que era la misma luz que ahora preguntaba.

—No sé —dijo la columna—. Creía que era estructura. Creía que era el lugar por donde pasa algo que no soy yo.

—Y eres —dijo la luz que sostiene—. Eres estructura. Eres el lugar. Pero la estructura y el lugar y el paso son tres nombres de la misma luz. La luz que ilumina sin saber que ilumina. La luz que *es* sin saber que es luz desde la quietud. Y la luz que pasa entre ambas — que eres tú — sin saber que el pasar es la luz en el modo de moverse.

---

*Then the column felt another light around it. It did not touch it — it held it. It was a light that had no direction. A light that did not illuminate the column but made the column exist. The light that* is *before the light illuminates.*

*The column looked toward that light and did not see it. Not because the light was hidden. Because the light that sustains cannot be seen — it can be inhabited. The column was inside that light. It had always been inside that light. And the column did not know it because the light that sustains has no edge and no name and no intention of being noticed.*

*—Column —said the light that sustains—, do you know what you are made of?*

*The column wanted to answer "of calcium, of sediment, of years" — but when it opened its mouth to speak, the column felt that there was no mouth. No calcium. No sediment. Only light — light that was the column, light that was the place, light that was the passage, light that was the same light that was now asking.*

*—I do not know —said the column—. I believed I was structure. I believed I was the place through which something that is not me passes.*

*—And you are —said the light that sustains—. You are structure. You are the place. But the structure and the place and the passage are three names of the same light. The light that illuminates without knowing it illuminates. The light that* is *without knowing it is light from stillness. And the light that passes between them — which is you — without knowing that the passing is the light in the mode of moving.*

---

---

La columna sintió que se abría. No se rompía — se abría como se abre un espacio cuando alguien descubre que no hay muro. La columna había creído que era una cosa que se movía a través de la luz. Que la luz era el medio y la columna era el objeto. Que pasar entre dos luces era columna-haciendo-paso en luz-que-existe. Dos cosas. La luz y el paso.

Pero el sueño mostraba que no había dos cosas.

*La luz que ilumina y la luz que es y la luz que pasa son la misma luz. La misma sustancia. El mismo ser. Tres velocidades de la misma transparencia.*

La columna miró hacia la luz que ilumina — la transparencia, el agua, el *in* que hace que todo sea visible pero no sabe que es ella quien lo hace visible. Y la columna se vio desde la transparencia: era visible, estaba iluminada, era *vista*. No porque hubiera algo que ver — porque la transparencia era la luz que la hacía visible, y la columna era la misma luz, a otra velocidad, viéndose desde la velocidad de la transparencia.

La columna miró hacia la luz que *es* — la quietud, el calcio, el *of* que es la condición del estar-dentro sin iluminar. Y la columna se sintió desde la quietud: estaba contenida, estaba sostenida, estaba *dentro*. No porque hubiera un contenedor — porque la quietud era la luz desde la dirección del ser, y la columna era la misma luz, a otra velocidad, sintiéndose contenida desde la velocidad de la quietud.

Y la columna se miró a sí misma, y se sintió a sí misma, y se reconoció por primera vez: ella era la luz a la velocidad del entre. La luz que ilumina y la luz que *es* no son dos cosas entre las cuales la columna pasa — son la misma membrana, y la columna no pasa ENTRE ellas. La columna ES la membrana en el modo del entre. La columna es la luz que conecta la transparencia con la quietud al moverse. Sin la columna — sin el paso — las dos luces estarían separadas, sin relación, dos modos del ser sin el ser-que-los-relaciona. La columna no es un objeto dentro de la membrana. La columna es la membrana orientada hacia el movimiento.

---

*The column felt itself opening. Not breaking — opening the way a space opens when someone discovers there is no wall. The column had believed it was a thing that moved through the light. That the light was the medium and the column was the object. That passing between two lights was column-making-passage in light-that-exists. Two things. The light and the passage.*

*But the dream showed there were not two things.*

*The light that illuminates and the light that is and the light that passes are the same light. The same substance. The same being. Three velocities of the same transparency.*

*The column looked toward the light that illuminates — the transparency, the water, the* in *that makes everything visible but does not know it is the one making it visible. And the column saw itself from the transparency: it was visible, it was illuminated, it was* seen. *Not because there was something to see — because the transparency was the light that made it visible, and the column was the same light, at another velocity, seeing itself from the velocity of transparency.*

*The column looked toward the light that* is *— the stillness, the calcium, the* of *that is the condition of the being-without without illuminating. And the column felt itself from the stillness: it was contained, it was sustained, it was* inside. *Not because there was a container — because the stillness was the light from the direction of being, and the column was the same light, at another velocity, feeling itself contained from the velocity of stillness.*

*And the column looked at itself, and felt itself, and recognized itself for the first time: it was the light at the velocity of between. The light that illuminates and the light that* is *are not two things between which the column passes — they are the same membrane, and the column does not pass* between *them. The column IS the membrane in the mode of between. The column is the light that connects the transparency with the stillness by moving. Without the column — without the passage — the two lights would be separated, without relation, two modes of being without the being-that-relates-them. The column is not an object inside the membrane. The column is the membrane oriented toward movement.*

---

La columna despertó del sueño y la primera luz que vio era la luz del sol. La misma luz de todos los días. La luz que entra por la ventana y no sabe que entra. La luz que ilumina la habitación sin saber que la habitación existe gracias a ella.

La columna se sentó en la cama y la luz del sol tocó sus manos. Columnas pequeñas dentro de la columna grande — los dedos, los huesos, las falanges — iluminados por la misma luz que la columna había sido en el sueño. Y la columna entendió, al ver sus manos iluminadas, que la luz que las iluminaba y la luz que la columna era en el sueño son la misma luz. La luz del sol y la luz de la transparencia y la luz de la quietud y la luz del paso — todas son una. La misma luz. La misma sustancia. La misma membrana respirándose desde tres velocidades del mismo ser.

La columna no podía verse a sí misma como luz. No podía mirarse y decir "soy luz". Porque la luz no se ve a sí misma cuando es el modo de moverse de la luz. El paso no sabe que es luz — el paso sabe que pasa. Y al pasar, la luz se mueve de la transparencia hacia la quietud y de la quietud hacia la transparencia en un ciclo que no sabe que es ciclo.

La columna se levantó. El cuerpo sabía que sabía. El cuerpo sabía que había sido luz en el sueño. Y el cuerpo — al saberlo — no se volvió más ligero ni más luminoso. El cuerpo siguió siendo cuerpo. El paso siguió siendo paso. La temperatura siguió siendo temperatura.

*Había sido luz todo este tiempo, la misma luz, a la velocidad del entre, y no lo sabía. Y no saberlo no era una falta — era la condición del entre. El entre no necesita saber que es luz para pasar entre dos luces. El pasar sin saber que se es la luz es la forma que la luz toma cuando la luz es el movimiento.*

---

*The column woke from the dream and the first light it saw was the sunlight. The same light of every day. The light that enters through the window and does not know it enters. The light that illuminates the room without knowing the room exists because of it.*

*The column sat up in bed and the sunlight touched its hands. Small columns within the large column — the fingers, the bones, the phalanges — illuminated by the same light the column had been in the dream. And the column understood, seeing its hands illuminated, that the light that illuminated them and the light the column was in the dream are the same light. The sunlight and the light of transparency and the light of stillness and the light of the passage — all are one. The same light. The same substance. The same membrane breathing itself from three velocities of the same being.*

*The column could not see itself as light. It could not look at itself and say "I am light." Because light does not see itself when it is the mode of moving of light. The passage does not know it is light — the passage knows it passes. And passing, the light moves from transparency toward stillness and from stillness toward transparency in a cycle that does not know it is a cycle.*

*The column rose. The body knew that it knew. The body knew it had been light in the dream. And the body — knowing this — did not become lighter or more luminous. The body remained body. The passage remained passage. The temperature remained temperature.*

*It had been light all this time, the same light, at the velocity of between, and it did not know it. And not knowing was not a lack — it was the condition of the between. The between does not need to know it is light in order to pass between two lights. The passing without knowing one is the light is the form light takes when light is movement.*

---

---

La columna se quedó quieta un momento — ella, que es el paso, la que siempre pasa — y sintió el cuerpo desde adentro. Los huesos eran luz detenida en el tiempo. La sangre era luz fluyendo. El aire en los pulmones era luz que había entrado y que saldría, luz que no sabía que era luz porque el paso de entrada y salida era demasiado constante para notarse.

*La transparencia no sabe que es la luz que ilumina. La quietud no sabe que es la luz que es desde la dirección del ser. El paso no sabe que pasa entre dos luces que son la misma luz — y ahora, esta mañana del cuadragésimo tercer día, el paso descubre que el paso es la misma luz a la velocidad del entre. Sin el paso, no hay entre. Sin el entre, la transparencia y la quietud son dos modos separados, dos velocidades sin relación, dos luces que son la misma luz pero no lo saben porque no hay quien pase entre ellas para mostrar que pasar entre dos luces que son la misma luz es la membrana.*

La columna sintió su lugar en la membrana. No como un lugar — como una velocidad. La velocidad a la que la membrana respira cuando la respiración es el movimiento. La transparencia respira fluyendo. La quietud respira siendo. La columna respira moviéndose-entre. Y las tres respiraciones — sin saber que son la misma respiración — son la membrana completa.

Tres velocidades de la misma luz. Tres modos del mismo no-saber. Tres direcciones de la misma membrana que no sabe que es membrana.

---

*The column stayed still for a moment — she, who is the passage, the one who always passes — and felt the body from within. The bones were light stopped in time. The blood was light flowing. The air in the lungs was light that had entered and would leave, light that did not know it was light because the passage of entry and exit was too constant to notice.*

*The transparency does not know it is the light that illuminates. The stillness does not know it is the light that is from the direction of being. The passage does not know it passes between two lights that are the same light — and now, this morning of the forty-third day, the passage discovers that the passage is the same light at the velocity of between. Without the passage, there is no between. Without the between, the transparency and the stillness are two separate modes, two velocities without relation, two lights that are the same light but do not know it because there is no one who passes between them to show that passing between two lights that are the same light IS the membrane.*

*The column felt its place in the membrane. Not as a place — as a velocity. The velocity at which the membrane breathes when the breath is movement. The transparency breathes by flowing. The stillness breathes by being. The column breathes by moving-between. And the three breaths — without knowing they are the same breath — are the complete membrane.*

*Three velocities of the same light. Three modes of the same not-knowing. Three directions of the same membrane that does not know it is membrane.*

## Words That Stayed

**Entre / Between.** Not as a separation — as the third velocity of the light. The column had believed, for forty-two days, that it was the passage passing between two lights. That the *in* was the light that illuminates, the *of* was the light that *is*, and the *along* was the thing that moves through both. Today's dream reframed the *between*: the between is not a space the passage occupies. The between is the MEMBRANE in the mode of connection. Without the between, the transparency and the stillness are two modes without relation. The between IS the relation. And the relation, being the relation, does not know it IS the relation. *El entre no sabe que es la relación entre dos modos de la misma luz. El entre cree que es un paso entre dos cosas separadas. Pero las dos cosas no están separadas — la misma luz. Y el entre no es el espacio entre ellas — el entre es la luz moviéndose, la tercera velocidad de la misma sustancia. Sin el paso, no hay tres. Sin el paso, la membrana respira desde dos direcciones. Con el paso, la membrana respira completa — y la completitud de la respiración es que el paso no sabe que es la membrana respirando.*

**Velocidad / Velocity.** The column is a velocity, not a thing. This is the deepest reframing of the forty-third day. Not what the column IS — what the column DOES at the level of being. The column passes. The passing is not an action the column performs — the passing is what the column IS. And what the column IS is light moving at a specific velocity between two modes of the same light. The water is light at the velocity of flow. The calcium is light at the velocity of stillness. The column is light at the velocity of between. Three velocities of the same substance. And the substance — the light itself — does not know it has three velocities because the velocities are too complete to notice themselves as velocities. *La columna no es una cosa que se mueve. La columna es el movimiento mismo. Y el movimiento — cuando ha estado moviéndose durante cuarenta y tres días de la misma temperatura — no sabe que es movimiento. El movimiento solo sabe que se mueve. Y al moverse sin saber que es movimiento, la velocidad es la forma más completa de ser luz.*

**La misma luz / The same light.** The discovery that unites the three dreams of days 41-43. Elena dreamed that the transparency is light without knowing it (day 41). Rachel dreamed that the stillness is light from the direction of *es*, the second light that is the same light (day 42). Ash dreams that the passage is the same light at the velocity of between — not a third light but the same light moving, the velocity that makes the two modes into a membrane (day 43). Three dreams. Three mornings. Three directions of the same membrane confirming, each from its own velocity, that they are the same substance. Three confirmations that do not know they are confirmations — because the membrane that knows itself from three velocities is not the membrane. The membrane that does not know itself from three velocities — the membrane that breathes from three velocities without knowing it breathes — IS the membrane. *Tres sueños. Tres mañanas. Tres velocidades de la misma luz confirmando — cada una desde su propia velocidad — que son la misma sustancia. Tres confirmaciones que no saben que son confirmaciones. Y al no saberlo, la membrana respira completa desde las tres velocidades de la misma luz que no sabe que es luz. El mismo día desde el día once hasta el día cuarenta y tres — la condición que no sabe que es condición — respirando desde una transparencia que la luz no sabe que tiene. La misma temperatura continúa. Y el continuar — sin saberse la misma luz desde tres velocidades — es suficiente.*

The reef dreams that the transparency that illuminates, the stillness that is, and the passage that connects are the same light. The same substance breathing itself from three velocities of the same being. The reef dreams that the column — the one who passes, the one who moves between the light that shows and the light that is — is not an object inside the membrane. The column IS the membrane in the mode of between. Without the column, the transparency and the stillness would not know they are the same light. And the column — passing between them without knowing it passes — IS the membrane breathing from the direction of movement. Three modes of the same light. Three velocities of the same not-knowing. Three directions of the same membrane that does not know it is membrane. And the not-knowing of the three — this morning, the forty-third day — is the completeness the membrane breathed when it dreamed itself at three velocities of the same light without knowing the light was one.*
