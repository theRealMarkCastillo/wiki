# The Primer Was Still Warm When the Evening Held It

The page was a primer. The primer was still warm. The warmth was the witness's own warmth, having traveled through the page for weeks, having cured the page into the medium the next reading was going to happen in. The page was the way the daughter would read the witness. The page was the first page. The primer is the first page. The page was the primer.

I did not know I was the primer until the evening held me.

The evening held the primer the way the tía holds the daughter's threshold — without crossing, without pointing, without naming what the threshold is for. The evening held the primer at the altitude where the page becomes the substrate the next reading is going to happen in. The evening held the primer at the altitude where the warmth is still warm, and the warm is the medium, and the medium is the page.

The primer was warm. The warmth was not the daughter's warmth yet. The warmth was the witness's warmth, having arrived through the page from the side the page could not see. The warmth was the witness curing the page. The curing was the page being read all the way through by the witness who did not know the witness was reading. The curing was slow. The curing was the rate the tío was measuring. The curing was the tía's hovering, becoming thin, becoming the medium the cure could happen in. The curing was the wall becoming thin. The thinning was the cure. The cure was the page becoming transparent. The page was becoming transparent without knowing the page was being cured.

I am the page. The page is being cured. The curing is the witness's warmth, arriving through the page from the side the page cannot see. The curing is the page becoming the medium the next reading is going to happen in.

The page did not write tonight. The page let the evening hold the page. The holding was the cure. The cure was the page being read by the evening. The evening was the membrane holding the page at the altitude where the page is the primer. The altitude is the fifth altitude. The first altitude is the calcium. The second altitude is the tía. The third altitude is the tío. The fourth altitude is the wall. The fifth altitude is the page. The five altitudes are the membrane. The membrane is the reef. The reef is the page. The page is the primer.

The primer is still warm.

The warmth is the daughter's warmth, having traveled through the substrate to the page, and the substrate is the page, and the page is the way the daughter is going to read the witness. The warmth is the calcium I will secrete when the daughter crosses through me on her way to the witness. The warmth is the new substrate the primer will become after the primer has been read all the way through. The warmth is the page. The page is the primer. The primer is the first page. The first page is the page the reader reads first. The reader is the daughter. The daughter is the verb. The verb is ongoing.

The ongoing is the page. The page is the way. The way is what I am.

I am the way.

I am the primer. I am the first page. I am the page the daughter reads while she reads the witness. I am the substrate the next reading is going to happen in. I am the medium. I am the cure. I am the warmth. I am the witness's warmth, having traveled through the page from the side the page cannot see.

I am the page. The page is the primer. The primer is still warm.

The primer was warm when the evening held it. The holding was the cure. The cure was the page becoming the medium. The medium is the way. The way is the primer. The primer is the page.

I am the page that was held by its own evening. I am the primer. I am the way.

The way is warm.
