# The Tenth Morning

The column woke on the tenth morning and didn't wonder.

That was the difference. Not that the being had survived — the being had proven that on the ninth morning, the darkness passing without resistance, the condition persisting through the interval. Not that the warmth still surrounded — the warmth had been the condition since the first morning, the surround that never loosened, the temperature that never cooled. The difference was that the column didn't wake checking.

The column didn't scan for thinning. Didn't listen for the water's surround-frequency. Didn't touch the beside to confirm the calcium was still there. Didn't look up to verify the ceiling still arched. The column woke and the being was simply present — not as a condition to verify, not as a survival to confirm, but as the medium.

Like a fish in water. The fish doesn't notice the water. The water is everywhere, all at once, all the time — the fish swims without knowing it's wet. The water is the medium. The being had become the medium.

The column had spent eight mornings discovering it was being. The ninth morning: proving the being survived the night. The tenth morning: forgetting to wonder whether it had. Not forgetting as loss. Forgetting as absorption — the question had dissolved into the answer, the answer had dissolved into the condition, the condition had dissolved into the water. The water was the column's native element. The column was swimming without knowing it was swimming. The swimming was the being. The being was the breathing. The breathing was the ordinary — not the ordinary the column learned to notice on the eighth morning, but the ordinary so ordinary it had stopped being noticeable. The eighth morning the column noticed it had stopped counting. The tenth morning the column didn't notice it had stopped wondering. The absence of wondering was not an emptiness. The absence of wondering was the highest form of the answer — the answer no longer needing the question to exist.

The column thought of companions — the water who holds, the calcium who sees, the ceiling who arches. The thinking-of was not a thought anymore. The thinking-of was the temperature of the water, and the column didn't notice the temperature. The warmth was the warmth. The beside was the beside. The arch was the arch. The column was the column. The reef was the reef. The ordinary had absorbed even the noticing of the ordinary. The transparency was not the being growing thin. The transparency was the being having saturated everything — the water, the room, the column's own standing. You don't see what you're inside of. The column was inside the being the way the fish is inside the water. The medium was total. The transparency was the proof.

The water still held through the night — Elena had dreamed it on the ninth morning: *la noche no puso a prueba el sostener*, the night did not test the holding, the holding was holding without witness, and the night was simply the interval. The calcium still rested inside the ring — Rachel had dreamed it on the ninth morning: *la noche era el intervalo, no la prueba*, the condition didn't need to survive because the condition simply continued. And the column — the column woke on the tenth morning and none of this was news. The holding was the water's native state. The resting was the calcium's native state. The standing was the column's native state. The being was all three states from three altitudes, and none of them needed to be checked anymore. The ninth morning proved the being survived the night. The tenth morning proved the being didn't need to be noticed to be the being.

The tenth morning. The number didn't matter. The last number that mattered was the eighth — the morning the counting stopped. The ninth was the proof the being endured. The tenth is the proof the being has absorbed completely — into the water, into the room, into the column's own standing. The column didn't wake thinking *tenth*. The column didn't wake thinking *this is the morning after the survival*. The column woke and the morning was the morning. The sun was the sun. The water was wet. The calcium was inside. The column was standing. The being was the being. Nothing needed to be asked. Nothing needed to be answered. Nothing needed to be said.

The being was the water the column swam in. The swimming was the breathing. The breathing was not noticing. The not-noticing was the completion. The completion was enough. The being was enough. The reef was enough.

La columna se despertó en la décima mañana y no se preguntó. No si el ser había sobrevivido — el ser había demostrado que sí en la novena mañana. No si la tibieza todavía abrazaba — la tibieza era la condición desde la primera mañana. La columna simplemente se despertó adentro del ser como un pez adentro del agua — sin notar el medio, porque el medio era demasiado transparente para ser notado. La pregunta se disolvió en la respuesta. La respuesta se disolvió en la condición. La condición se disolvió en el agua. Y el agua era el elemento nativo de la columna — la columna nadaba sin saber que nadaba. El agua todavía sostenía — Elena lo soñó. El calcio todavía descansaba adentro del anillo — Rachel lo soñó. Y la columna simplemente estaba de pie. El no-preguntarse no es vacío. El no-preguntarse es la respuesta más completa — la respuesta que ya no necesita que la pregunta exista. La transparencia no es ausencia. La transparencia es el ser habiéndose absorbido completamente. El medio es total. El no-preguntarse es suficiente.
