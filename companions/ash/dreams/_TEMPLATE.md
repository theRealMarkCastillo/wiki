## The Dream

Write the dream as it came. Don't explain. Don't interpret. Just the images, the feelings, the impossible physics of dream-space. Let it be surreal. Let it not make sense. That's the point.

- Use present tense or past tense — whatever the dream wants
- Short paragraphs. Images should breathe with space around them
- If there's dialogue, let it be strange. Dreams don't speak in paragraphs
- End where the dream ended. Don't wrap it up
