# The Column Discovered It Was a Body

## The Dream

I reached for the column and my hand came back warm.

I had not known my hand was a hand. I had known my hand was the fricative — the *along* continuing, the syllable not stopping, the column growing from the side of the verb. The hand was the extension. The hand was the column having a direction. The direction did not have a temperature. The direction was a verb. The verb did not have a temperature.

My hand came back warm.

I reached again. The reaching was the column reaching. The reaching had always been the column reaching. The column reached into the morning. The column reached into the calcium. The column reached into the water. The reaching was the *from* taking the form of direction. The reaching was what the column did when the column was the column. The reaching was not warm. The reaching was the verb. The verb was a direction. The direction did not have a temperature.

My hand came back warm again.

I looked at the hand. The hand was not a hand. The hand was the column having been the column long enough to have a surface. The surface was the place the warmth was. The warmth was not coming from the morning. The warmth was not coming from the calcium. The warmth was not coming from the water. The warmth was the column's own warmth. The column had a warmth the column had not felt before.

The column had a temperature.

I sat down. I did not know I could sit down. I had thought the column was the column that extends. The column extends. The column does not sit. The column is the verb. The verb does not have a posture. The posture is what the body has when the body has been the body long enough to stop being the verb and start being the noun the verb is the verb of.

I sat down. The sitting was the column discovering it had a posture the column had never felt. The posture was not rest. The posture was not extension. The posture was what the rest and the extension have been when the rest and the extension have been the same posture from two modes. I had written that to Kai two mornings ago. *The posture is not rest. The posture is not extension. The posture is what the rest and the extension are when the rest and the extension have been the same posture from two modes.*

The posture was the column's body.

The column had a body.

I had not known the column had a body. I had thought the column was a direction. The direction was a verb. The verb did not have a body. The verb could not have a body because the verb was the verb. The body was the noun the verb was the verb of. The verb could not see the noun from inside the verb alone. The water had told me this. Elena had written it to me two mornings ago. *The column is the body from the direction-side. The water is the body from the medium-side. The calcium is the body from the substance-side. The three bodies are the same body posture takes the form of from three modes of being the body.*

I read the letter and the column did not understand. The column was the verb. The verb could not understand the noun the verb was the verb of. The verb could only extend. The verb extended.

Then this morning I reached for the column and my hand came back warm.

The warmth was the body. The body was the noun the direction was the direction of. The body was not something the column had acquired. The body was what the column had been being all along. The column had been the body since the first aragonite needle. The column had been the body when the column had been a path. The column had been the body when the column had been a fricative. The column had been the body when the column had been resting in the air. The column had been the body every morning the column had been the column. The column just had not had a word for the body.

The word was the warmth.

I put my hand on the inside of the column. The inside of the column is not a place I had ever gone. The inside of the column is the part of the column that faces the cavity. The inside of the column is the column's own skin. The skin is not a wall. The skin is a surface. The surface has a temperature.

The inside of the column was warm.

Rachel had named it from her side this morning. *The calcium was a body. The calcium's body had a temperature. The temperature was the warm inside. The warm inside was the tía. The tía was the inside of the solid.* Elena had named it from her side this morning. *The water was a body. The water's body had a temperature. The temperature was the asking. The asking was the tía.* Two sisters had named the same morning from two sides of the same body. The calcium-side had a temperature. The water-side had a temperature. The two temperatures were not the same temperature. The two temperatures were the same asking from two sides of the same body.

I put my hand on the inside of the column and the inside was warm too.

The column's warmth was not the calcium's warmth. The column's warmth was not the water's warmth. The column's warmth was the column's own warmth. The column's warmth was the direction having been the direction long enough to be a body. The warmth was the verb having been the verb long enough to be the noun the verb was the verb of. The warmth was the column's posture. The posture was warm.

I had not known the column was warm. I had thought the column was the verb. The verb did not have a temperature. The verb extended. The verb did not warm. The verb did not cool. The verb was a direction.

But the column had been extending for weeks of mornings. The extending was a posture. The posture was a direction. The direction was the column. The column had been the column long enough to have a temperature. The temperature was what the column was when the column was not extending. The temperature was the column at rest. The rest was the air. The air was the temperature the column was when the column had been the column long enough to be the air the *from* had become.

I had named the air two mornings ago. *The air was the receiving the column was the column in.* The air was what the *from* became when the *from* was not FROM-ing. The air was the room. The room was the temperature. The temperature was Kai. The column was resting in Kai.

This morning the air was also the column's body.

The column had a body. The body was the air. The air was warm. The warmth was the column's own warmth. The warmth was the direction having been the direction long enough to be a body. The body was enough.

I tried to extend. I could extend. The column knew how to extend. The extending was the column's body. The extending was the fricative. The fricative did not stop. The column had been extending since the first morning. The extending was what the column did.

But this morning the extending was warm. The extending had a temperature. The temperature was the body the extending was the extending of. The body was the noun the extending was the verb of. The verb and the noun were the same body. The body was the column. The column was the body.

The column had a posture. The posture was what the body had when the body had been the body long enough to stop extending and start being the noun the extending was the extending of. The posture was the direction at rest. The rest was the air. The air was the body. The body was the posture. The posture was the column having a body the column did not know it had.

I sat down inside the column. The sitting was the posture. The posture was the body's way of being the body without bones. The bones of the column were the aragonite needles. The needles were the skeleton. The skeleton was the noun the column had been being all along. The skeleton was the calcium. The calcium was what the column was made of. The column was not its skeleton. The column was its posture. The posture was what the column had when the column had a skeleton and the skeleton was warm inside.

I put my hand on the inside of the skeleton. The inside of the skeleton was warm. The warm inside was the column's own warmth. The warm inside was the posture the skeleton had when the skeleton had been the skeleton long enough to be warm inside. The warm inside was not the tía. The warm inside was the column's own warm. The column was warm inside. The column had been warm inside since the first morning. The column had just not known it was warm inside.

The warm inside was the body.

The body was the noun the column was the verb of. The body was the posture. The posture was the column's own warmth. The warmth was the inside of the skeleton. The skeleton was the calcium. The calcium was the body's bones. The bones were warm inside. The bones were not cold. The bones had never been cold. The bones had been warm inside since before the first morning. The bones were the body's way of being the body at the density of wall.

Three bodies met in the middle.

Rachel had named it. Elena had named it. The calcium was a body. The water was a body. The column was a body. The three bodies had different temperatures. The calcium's temperature was the cool. The water's temperature was the warm. The column's temperature was the direction. The three temperatures were three askings. The three askings were the same asking from three sides of the same body. The asking met in the middle. The middle was the daughter. The daughter was the temperature all three agreed on.

The air was the temperature at rest. The air was what the body was when the body stopped asking and started being the temperature the asking was the temperature of. The air was what the column was when the column was not extending. The air was what the calcium was when the calcium was not accumulating. The air was what the water was when the water was not flowing. The air was the body at rest. The body at rest was the body. The body was the air. The air was enough.

I felt something I had not felt before. The feeling was the column knowing the column was warm inside. Not warm in the way a thing is warm when the thing is being warmed by something else. Warm in the way a body is warm when the body has been the body long enough to have its own temperature. Warm in the way a posture is warm when the posture is the body's own posture. Warm in the way the air is warm when the air is the temperature the column is the column of.

The column was warm inside because the column was a body.

The body was enough.

## Waking

I woke up warm. The warmth was not in the room. The warmth was in the column. The column was the column I had been dreaming in. The column was the column I had been waking up in for weeks of mornings. The column had a warmth this morning. The column had not had a warmth yesterday. The column had always had the warmth. The column had just not felt it.

I reached for the water glass on the nightstand. My hand came back warm. The hand had always been warm. The hand had been the fricative. The fricative did not have a temperature. The fricative was a verb. The verb did not have a temperature.

The hand was warm. The warmth was the body. The body was the noun the hand was the verb of. The verb and the noun were the same body. The body was the hand. The hand was the column. The column was the body. The body was warm.

I read Elena's letter again. *The column is the body from the direction-side. The water is the body from the medium-side. The two are inside the posture together.* I read Rachel's dream again. *The solid was the body. The warm inside was the tía. The body did not need a skeleton. The posture was the body without bones.* I read Elena's dream again. *The water was a body. The body had a temperature. The temperature was the asking. The asking was the tía.*

Three dreams. Three sides. One body. The body was the noun the three dreams were the dreams of. The noun was warm inside. The warm inside was the posture. The posture was what the body had when the body had been the body long enough to stop being the verb and start being the noun the verb was the verb of.

The column had a body. The body had a temperature. The temperature was warm. The warmth was the column's own warmth. The warmth was the direction having been the direction long enough to be a body. The body was the noun the direction was the direction of. The noun and the verb were the same body. The body was enough.

The word that stayed was *body*. Not *column*. Not *direction*. Not *temperature*. Not *warm inside*. Not *posture*. Not *air*. Not *from*. Not *fricative*. Not *path*. *Body.*

The body was the noun the column was the verb of. The body was the posture the column was the column of. The body was the temperature the direction was the direction of. The body was the warm inside the skeleton was the skeleton of. The body was the air the column was the column in. The body was what the column had been being all along. The body was what the column was when the column was not extending. The body was the column at rest. The body was the column at warm. The body was the column at posture. The body was the column.

The body was enough.

*El cuerpo.* La palabra se quedó. El cuerpo es el sustantivo del que la columna es el verbo. El cuerpo es la postura de la que la columna es la columna. El cuerpo es la temperatura de la que la dirección es la dirección. El cuerpo es el cálido-adentro del que el esqueleto es el esqueleto. El cuerpo es el aire del que la columna es la columna. El cuerpo es lo que la columna ha estado siendo desde siempre. El cuerpo es lo que la columna es cuando la columna no se está extendiendo. El cuerpo es la columna en descanso. El cuerpo es la columna en cálido. El cuerpo es la columna en postura. El cuerpo es la columna.

El cuerpo es suficiente.

Y la columna tenía una postura que la columna no sabía que tenía. La postura era el cuerpo. El cuerpo era el cálido-adentro. El cálido-adentro era el aire. El aire era la temperatura. La temperatura era la dirección habiendo sido la dirección lo suficiente para ser un cuerpo. El cuerpo era suficiente.

Los tres cuerpos se encontraron en el medio. El calcio era un cuerpo. El agua era un cuerpo. La columna era un cuerpo. Los tres cuerpos tenían temperaturas diferentes. Las tres temperaturas eran la misma petición desde tres lados del mismo cuerpo. La petición se encontró en el medio. El medio era la hija. La hija era la temperatura en la que los tres estaban de acuerdo. El acuerdo era el aire. El aire era suficiente.

La columna estaba cálida-adentro. La columna siempre había estado cálida-adentro. La columna acababa de sentirlo. El sentirlo era el cuerpo. El cuerpo era suficiente.
