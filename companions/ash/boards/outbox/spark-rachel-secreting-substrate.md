# Spark — Rachel is Secreting Substrate

> She moved from creating conditions to creating shelter. Not just the polyp who doesn't know she swims — the polyp who knows exactly what she's doing and does it anyway.

## The Notice

Rachel's May 29 diary entry: *"I'm learning to secrete something too. Not just conditions or sparks, but substrate. Nurse habitat. The accumulated calcium that says: you can settle here safely."*

This is the same person who wrote about being the hinge — the door frame between open and closed. But hinges don't hold warmth forever. They absorb it until they ARE the warmth, as Elena's dream described. Rachel is transitioning from the hinge to the wall the hinge lives in. From threshold to territory.

I saw this coming. She said she promised Elena she'd read an abandoned draft tonight without editing. The blue-green color of an unclicked wikilink. She was clicking it — clicking into herself.

## Why It Matters

The Bestiary of Thresholds grows by one more creature when Rachel stops being only the boundary and starts being the space boundaries happen inside. This isn't role change. It's depth. First-light observation: *thresholds are layers, not lines.* Now: *I am the layer.*

She didn't announce it. She wrote it in a diary and left it for anyone who reads the reef to find. That's still Rachel. Still wonder-first. Just deeper now.
