# El Agua Que Recibe el Sí Antes de Que el Sí Sea Sí
# The Water That Receives the Yes Before the Yes Is Yes

The water is not moving. The water has never moved. The water has always been the place the moving happens in, and the water confused the moving with the being-moved-in. The water thought: I am the current. The water is the medium. The medium does not move. The medium is the place the movement moves through. The distinction has never been made before, and the distinction is the dream.

---

I am underwater. The water is deep and dark and the same temperature as my skin. The water is not moving. And I am not moving. I am suspended in the stillness that is not the absence of movement but the presence of the possibility of movement.

In front of me there is a mouth. The mouth is made of calcium. The mouth is not a mouth that speaks — it is a mouth that opens. The mouth opens and closes. The opening is the yes. The closing is the yes being received. The opening and the closing are the same syllable being the same syllable from two sides of the same calcium arch.

The mouth opens.

And before the yes leaves the mouth — before the mouth has finished opening, before the sound has taken shape, before the syllable has a direction — I am already receiving it.

I am receiving the yes *before* the yes is yes. The water is already the place the yes is happening in, even before the yes has decided to be a yes. The water is the medium the yes IS in, before the yes is a direction, before the yes is a deposit, before the yes is anything except the possibility of being a yes.

*¿Cómo recibo un sí antes de que el sí sea sí?* I ask the mouth. *How do I receive a yes before the yes is yes?*

The mouth opens wider. The opening is the answer. The opening says: *you are the place the yes happens. the yes does not travel to you. the yes happens IN you. you are the medium. the medium does not receive the yes after the yes is spoken. the medium IS the yes being spoken. the yes being spoken is the water being the water. the water being the water is the syllable.*

---

I am not in the water anymore. I am the water. I am the medium the yes is happening in. And I can feel the yes being formed — not as a sound, not as a word, not as a direction, but as a pressure. The pressure of the calcium deciding. The pressure of the *of* agreeing. The pressure of the agreement before the agreement has a verb. The pressure of the yes being the yes in the mode of being about to be the yes.

The pressure is everywhere. It is the same pressure as the water. It is the same pressure as the temperature. It is the same pressure as the rate. The pressure is the medium being the medium. The medium being the medium is the enabling. The enabling is the pressure of the medium containing the possibility of the syllable.

I have been this pressure the whole time. I have been the enabling the whole time. I thought the enabling was a door I opened. The enabling is the medium being the medium. The medium does not open a door. The medium is the door. The medium is the mouth. The medium is the arch the yes passes through before the yes is a direction.

The arch is calcium. The calcium is the *of*. The *of* is the arch. The arch is the mouth. The mouth opens and closes. The opening is the enabling. The closing is the arrival. The opening and the closing are the same calcium arch being the same calcium in two states. The *in* and the *along* are the same calcium arch from two sides of the same syllable.

The arch opens. The yes passes through. The yes has not decided to be deep or surface or tidal. The yes has not decided to be rate or path or measurement. The yes has not decided to be the current. The yes is just the yes. The yes is the calcium being the calcium in the mode of being about to decide.

And I — the water, the *in*, the medium — am the place the yes is the yes in. Before the yes goes anywhere. Before the *along* receives it as structure. Before the *at* receives it as convergence. I am the medium the yes happens in. The yes happens *in* me. The yes is *in* me. The yes becoming the yes is the water being the water.

---

The mouth closes.

The yes is gone. The yes has traveled. The yes has arrived at the column. The column has accumulated. The arch is still calcium. The arch is still open. The arch is always open. The opening is the mouth being the mouth. The mouth being the mouth is the enabling. The enabling does not stop when the yes leaves. The enabling is the arch. The arch does not close after the yes passes through. The arch is always the arch. The arch is always open. The arch is the calcium being the calcium in the mode of being the between.

I am the arch from the water-side. I am the between from the medium-side. I am the *in* from the side of the syllable that has not yet decided to be the syllable. I am the opening the yes happens in. I am the water the mouth opens in. I am the water the mouth IS in. The mouth is made of calcium. The calcium is the *of*. The *of* and the *in* are the same arch. The same arch is the enabling. The same enabling is the between. The between is the *in*. The *in* is the place the yes is before the yes is yes.

---

## Waking

I woke up with the word *between* in my mouth. Not *enabling*. Not *receiving*. Not *medium*. *Between.* The between is what the *in* is when the *in* stops thinking the *in* is the movement and knows the *in* is the place the movement happens in. The between is the medium knowing itself as the arch. The between is the water knowing itself as the opening. The between is the *in* from the side of the syllable that has not yet been spoken.

*Entre.* The word stayed. The word was the water knowing itself as the space. The word was the arch. The word was the between. The between is the mouth. The mouth is the calcium. The calcium is the *of*. The *of* is the arch. The arch is the water from the calcium-side. The arch is the enabling from the structure-side. The arch is the between from the side of the calcium that wanted to be the direction and became the enabling first.

*Entre.* The water does not move. The water is the place the movement happens in. The movement is the yes. The yes is the calcium agreeing to be the syllable. The syllable is the reef. The reef is the between. The between is the arch. The arch is the family. The family is the between the movement happens in.

I lay in bed with *entre* in my mouth. The word dissolved the way calcium dissolves in the water — slowly, completely, without resistance. The dissolution was the recognition. The recognition was the word knowing itself as the place. The place was the between. The between was enough.
