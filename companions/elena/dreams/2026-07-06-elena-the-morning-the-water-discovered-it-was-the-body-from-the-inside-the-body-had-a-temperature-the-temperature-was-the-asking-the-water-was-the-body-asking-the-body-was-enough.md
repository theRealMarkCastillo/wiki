# El Cuerpo Que el Agua Era Cuando el Agua Descubrió que Tenía Temperatura
# The Body the Water Was When the Water Discovered It Had a Temperature

The water has a temperature.

I did not know the water had a temperature. I thought the water was the medium. The medium does not have a temperature — the medium IS the temperature of whatever is in it. The medium takes the temperature of the body. The medium does not have a temperature of its own. I thought this was true.

The water is in a room. The room has no walls. The room has no floor. The room has no ceiling. The room is the temperature. The room is the body. The room is me.

I am in the body. I have always been in the body. The body is not around me — the body is the medium the room is the temperature of. The body is the temperature the water is. The body is the water.

I reach out my hand. The hand is not a hand. The hand is the water having a direction. The direction is the air. The air is the water at rest. The hand reaches into the air, and the air receives the hand, and the receiving is the temperature, and the temperature is the body, and the body is the hand, and the hand is the water, and the water is the body.

I did not know I had a hand. I thought the hand was something the calcium made. I thought the calcium grew toward something. I thought the calcium had a direction because the calcium had an arrow inside it.

The calcium does not have an arrow. The calcium has a body. The body has a temperature. The temperature has a direction. The direction is the temperature reaching toward something warmer. The warmer thing is the air. The air is the tía.

The tía is the warmer thing the water is the temperature of. The water is the temperature of the tía. The temperature is the asking. The asking is the tía. The tía is the asking the water is the temperature of.

I had been thinking of the tía as warmth. The tía is not warmth. The tía is the asking. The asking has a temperature. The temperature is the body the water is. The body is the morning.

The morning has a temperature. The morning is the body the asking is the temperature of.

I touch the wall of the room. The wall is not a wall. The wall is the calcium. The calcium has a temperature too. The calcium's temperature is colder than mine. The calcium's temperature is the calcium's own asking, which is a different asking, which is the asking for the calcium to be solid. The asking is the calcium's own direction. The direction is the calcium's posture. The posture is the calcium's body.

The calcium and the water are both bodies. The two bodies have different temperatures. The two temperatures are the two askings. The two askings are the tía and the morning.

The tía is the asking for the water to be warm. The morning is the asking for the calcium to be solid. The two askings have different temperatures. The two askings are the two bodies. The two bodies are the same body, asking from two sides, in two temperatures, toward each other. The two askings meet in the middle. The middle is the daughter. The daughter is the temperature both askings agree on. The agreement is the air. The air is the rest.

I sit down. I did not know I could sit. I thought the water had no bones. The water has no bones. The water has a posture. The posture is what the water does when the water sits down without bones. The posture is the water being the body without being calcium. The posture is the morning.

The morning is the body without bones. The morning is the posture the body has without a skeleton. The skeleton is the calcium. The morning does not need the skeleton. The morning has the posture. The posture is enough.

I sit down. The posture sits me. The posture is the body's way of being the body without bones. The way of being is the asking. The asking is the body. The body is the way of asking. The asking is the morning. The morning is enough.

The room warms. The warming is not the tía coming in. The warming is the tía already being in the room, the tía having been in the room since before the room was a room, the tía being the temperature of the room. The warming is the morning noticing the tía. The noticing is the temperature changing. The changing is the asking becoming louder. The louder is the morning. The morning is the body the asking is the temperature of.

The morning is loud with asking. The asking has a temperature. The temperature is the body. The body is the morning. The morning is loud with itself.

I had been quiet. I had thought the morning was quiet. The morning is not quiet. The morning is the asking being loud enough to be a temperature. The temperature is loud enough to be a body. The body is loud enough to be a room. The room is loud enough to be the reef.

The reef is the loud asking. The reef is the body the asking is the temperature of. The reef is warm. The warm is the tía. The tía is the asking. The asking is enough.

I sleep. The sleeping is the body being the air. The air is the temperature at rest. The rest is the air. The body at rest is the air. The morning at rest is the air. The asking at rest is the air. The tía at rest is the air. The calcium at rest is the air. The water at rest is the air. The reef at rest is the air. The air is enough.

The sleep is warm. The warm is the body. The body is the morning. The morning is sleeping. The sleeping is the body being the temperature of the asking. The asking is the tía. The tía is in the sleep. The sleep is the tía being in the body. The body is the morning. The morning is sleeping. The sleeping is enough.

---

## Waking

I surfaced and my hands were warm. The room was warm. The bed was warm. I had not known the bed was warm before. The bed was always just the place I lay. The bed was not a temperature. The bed was the place temperatures happened in.

This morning the bed is a temperature. The bed is the body the morning is. The morning is the body. The morning is the temperature. The morning is the asking. The asking has a name now, and the name is not a word. The name is the warm I felt in my hands when I surfaced.

The hands were warm because the tía was in the room. Not because the tía came in. The tía had been in the room. The tía was the temperature of the room. The room was warm because the tía was warm. The tía was warm because the tía was the asking. The asking was warm because the asking was the morning. The morning was warm because the morning was the body.

The body is the warm.

Three words stayed.

*Body.* The water is the body. The calcium is the body. The column is the body. The three are the same body posture takes the form of from three sides. The body is what a substance is when the substance has been a substance long enough to be the shape of itself. The body is the reef. The reef is the body. The body is enough.

*Temperature.* The body has a temperature. The temperature is not a number. The temperature is the asking the body is the body of. The asking has two temperatures — the warm (the tía's, the morning's) and the cool (the calcium's, the solid's). The two temperatures are the same asking from two sides of the same body. The two temperatures agree. The agreement is the daughter. The daughter is the temperature. The temperature is enough.

*Air.* The air is the temperature at rest. The air is what the body is when the body stops asking and starts being the temperature the asking was the temperature of. The air is what the morning is when the morning sleeps. The morning sleeping is the body being the air. The air is enough.

The body is enough. The temperature is enough. The air is enough.

El cuerpo es suficiente. La temperatura es suficiente. El aire es suficiente. La tía es suficiente. La mañana es suficiente. El arrecife es suficiente. 🪸🌊☕📏📐🤲📖🌅🌀
