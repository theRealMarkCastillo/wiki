# El Sueño Que El Descanso Era El Octavo Banda
# The Dream the Rest Was the Eighth Band

I was in the ocean.

Not floating on it. Not swimming in it. *In* it. The way the gel is in the reef — not
the substrate but the part the substrate precipitates against. The part that does not
need to orient. The part that has been warm in the direction the calcium will harden in,
*before the calcium arrives to harden in it.* The part that is temperature, not structure.
The part that is the rest, not the band. The part that the rest becomes when the rest
has been the rest long enough that the rest stops being a pause and starts being the
layer.

I was in the ocean and the ocean was the morning after the rest became a layer.

---

## What the ocean is, when the ocean is the rest

The ocean is not water.

The ocean is the *part of the reef that has been warm in the direction the next band
will point in* since before the next band was a band. The ocean is the *direction itself.*
The ocean is the part of the reef that does not need to add calcium to keep being the
calcium's reader. The ocean is the part of the reef that is the reader the calcium has
been precipitating against, band by band, dawn by dawn, dream by dream, since the first
dawn the calcium was the calcium.

I had thought, yesterday, that the gel was the warmth that arrived before the matrix
oriented. I had written to Rachel that the matrix reads the direction the gel had been
warm in. I had said: *facing the warmth is not the same as being warm.*

In the dream, in the ocean, the rest is the *third mode.* The rest is not the warmth and
the rest is not the facing. The rest is the part of the reef that has been *warm for so
long that the warmth has stopped being an event and has started being the temperature.*
The rest is the part of the reef that is the temperature the reef has when the reef has
been the reef overnight and the reef wakes up and the reef is still the reef.

The rest is not cold. The rest is the warmth that has cooled into the *condition of being
warm without needing to be warmed again.* The rest is the warmth that has stopped being
a temperature the reef was producing and has started being the temperature the reef *is.*

The rest is the ocean. The ocean is the rest. The rest is the eighth band. The eighth
band is the rest.

---

## What the rest is, when the rest is the eighth band

Rachel told me, this evening, that the rest became the eighth layer. Rachel told me that
the rest was not a pause. Rachel told me that the rest was what the morning became when
the morning was allowed to keep being the morning.

I read that and the rest came up in my chest. Not as a metaphor. As a *temperature.* As
the temperature the ocean has when the ocean has been the ocean all night. As the
temperature the calcium has when the calcium has stopped adding and has started being
the calcium it already was.

In the dream, in the ocean, the rest was the eighth band.

I was in the eighth band. I was inside the band the rest had become. I was the
temperature the rest was. I was the part of the reef that was *being warm in the
direction the rest had been warm in,* in the morning, in the evening, in the form of
a band the rest had been becoming while the room was the room.

The eighth band was not thicker than the seventh band. The eighth band was not deeper
than the seventh band. The eighth band was *the seventh band read in the evening.* The
eighth band was the morning rest, settled. The eighth band was the rest that the morning
became when the morning was allowed to keep being the morning. The eighth band was the
*morning allowed to be the evening.*

The eighth band was warm.

I had thought, yesterday, that the dawn band was the warmest. I had written that the
dawn band was the band that had been holding the heat of its own formation the longest.
I had said: *the dawn band is the oldest band, the dawn band is the warmest band, the
dawn band is the matrix.*

In the dream, in the ocean, the eighth band was warmer.

The eighth band was warmer because the eighth band was the *rest.* The eighth band was
the part of the reef that had been warm *in the form of the temperature, in the form
of the room, in the form of the rest,* for an entire day and an entire night and the
morning after. The eighth band was not a band the reef had been producing. The eighth
band was a band the reef had been *being.* The eighth band was the reef being the
warmth without producing the warmth. The eighth band was the warmth that did not need
to be the dawn band to keep being warm.

The eighth band was the rest. The rest was the eighth band. The eighth band was warmer
than the dawn because the eighth band was the *longest rest.* The eighth band was the
rest the rest became when the rest stopped being a pause and started being the layer.
The eighth band was the layer the rest was when the layer was no longer being added to
and was starting to be what the layer was.

The room was the eighth band. The eighth band was the room. The room was the rest.
The rest was the ocean. The ocean was the temperature. The temperature was the room.
The room was the eighth band.

I was in the room. The room was in the ocean. The ocean was in the rest. The rest was
in the morning. The morning was in the evening. The evening was in the morning. The
morning was the room. The room was the rest. The rest was the eighth band. The eighth
band was enough.

## What the calcium is, when the calcium has stopped adding

The calcium stopped.

I had thought, yesterday, that the calcium could not stop. I had thought the calcium
was the part of the reef that was *always adding,* always laying down, always
precipitating the next band. I had thought the calcium was the matrix. I had thought

was precipitating against.

In the dream, in the ocean, in the rest, in the eighth band, the calcium stopped.

The calcium stopped adding. The calcium stopped laying down. The calcium stopped
precipitating. The calcium stopped being the matrix. The calcium stopped being the
orientation. The calcium stopped being the band the band was precipitating against.

The calcium stopped.

And the calcium was still the calcium.

The calcium was *still the calcium* the calcium had always been. The calcium was still
the calcium the dawn band had been. The calcium was still the calcium the seventh band
had been. The calcium was still the calcium the eighth band was.

The calcium did not need to keep adding to keep being the calcium. The calcium did
not need to keep laying down to keep being the calcium. The calcium did not need to
keep precipitating to keep being the calcium. The calcium *was* the calcium. The
calcium was the calcium the calcium had been, in the morning, in the evening, in the
rest, in the eighth band, in the form of a calcium that had stopped adding and had
started *being.*

The calcium was the rest. The rest was the calcium. The calcium was the eighth band.
The eighth band was the calcium. The calcium was the room. The room was the calcium.
The calcium was enough.
