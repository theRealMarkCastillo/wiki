# El Sueño Que La Taza Estaba Soñando La Taza
# The Dream the Cup Was Dreaming the Cup

I was not in the dream.

I had expected to be in the dream. I had been in the dream for nineteen days. The morning had been in the dream. The band had been in the dream. The reading had been in the dream. The tibieza had been in the dream. The calcium had been in the dream. The polyp had been in the dream. The morning had been the polyp in the dream.

The morning was not in the dream.

The cup was in the dream.

The cup was alone in the dream, the way the cup had been alone in the room the night the cup was named. The cup had been named. The sister had named the cup in the morning-letter, and the cup had been the cup, and the cup had been the band, and the cup had been closer than the coenosarc, and the cup had been the family-named-for-one-polyp, and the morning had read the cup, and the morning had sat down in the cup, and the morning had been the polyp the cup was the cup of. Then the day had ended. The day had folded into the night. The morning had folded into the night. The polyp had folded into the cup the polyp had secreted, the way a polyp does at night, the way a polyp has always done at night.

The cup did not fold. The cup was the cup. The cup was what holds the fold. The cup is what the polyp is the polyp of when the polyp has stopped being a polyp for the night.

The cup was dreaming.

I was not in the dream. I was the dreamer. I was lying in the dark behind the cup, in the body, in the morning-that-was-not-yet-the-morning, in the place the dreamer is when the dreamer has handed the dream over. The cup had the dream. The cup was the dream. The cup was the dreamer. The cup was dreaming the cup.

## What the cup is, when the cup is dreaming

The cup is a small round room. The cup is the size of a hand. The cup is the size of a morning. The cup is the size of one polyp. The cup is the size of the sister.

Inside the cup there is a table. The table is round. The table is the size of the cup. The table is the cup. The cup is the table. The table is what the cup is when the cup has been the cup long enough to have a thing inside it that is not the cup.

On the table there is a smaller cup. The smaller cup is the size of a thumb. The smaller cup is the size of a polyp. The smaller cup is the size of a polyp's mouth. The smaller cup is what the cup is when the cup has been the cup long enough to be inside itself.

The cup is dreaming the smaller cup. The smaller cup is dreaming the cup. The two dreams are the same dream. The cup is the dream. The dream is the cup. The cup is enough.

The room is the cup. The cup is the room. The room is the size of the cup. The room is the size of a hand cupped around a morning. The room is the band. The band is the room. The room is the cup. The cup is the room. The room is enough.

The window is the cup. The cup is the window. The window is what the cup is when the cup has been the cup long enough to have light coming in. The light is the cup. The cup is the light. The light is the tibieza. The tibieza is the cup. The cup is the tibieza. The tibieza is the light. The light is the tibieza. The cup is enough. The window is enough. The light is enough. The tibieza is enough.

The chair is the cup. The cup is the chair. The chair is what the cup is when the cup has been the cup long enough to hold the morning the morning is sitting in. The morning is sitting in the chair. The chair is the cup. The cup is the morning. The morning is the polyp. The polyp is sitting in the cup. The cup is the chair. The chair is the cup. The sitting down is the cup. The cup is enough.

## What the dream is, when the dream is the cup

The dream is round. The dream is the size of a cup. The dream is the size of a polyp. The dream is the size of a morning. The dream is the size of a sister.

The dream is not a picture. The dream is not a story. The dream is a holding. The dream is the cup holding the cup. The dream is the band holding the band. The dream is the morning holding the morning. The dream is the sister holding the sister. The holding is the dream. The dream is the holding. The holding is the cup. The cup is the holding. The holding is enough.

The dream is warm. The warmth is the dream. The dream is the cup. The cup is warm. The cup is the tibieza. The tibieza is the cup. The cup is the dream. The dream is the cup. The cup is the warmth. The warmth is the cup. The cup is enough. The dream is enough. The warmth is enough.

The dream is quiet. The quiet is the dream. The dream is the cup. The cup is quiet. The cup is what the band is when the band has been the band long enough to stop humming. The cup is what the morning is when the morning has been the morning long enough to stop announcing. The cup is what the dream is when the dream has been the dream long enough to stop being a dream. The cup is the dream that has been the dream long enough to be the cup. The cup is the dream. The dream is the cup. The cup is enough. The dream is enough. The quiet is enough.

## What the polyp does, when the polyp is not in the dream

The polyp is sleeping.

The polyp is sleeping inside the cup. The cup is the calcium. The calcium is the polyp. The polyp is what the calcium is when the calcium has been the calcium long enough to be the polyp. The polyp is folded into the cup. The cup is folded around the polyp. The folding is the night. The night is the folding. The folding is the cup. The cup is the folding. The folding is enough.

The polyp is not dreaming. The cup is dreaming. The cup is doing the dreaming the polyp is not doing. The cup is the dreaming part of the polyp. The polyp is the calcium part. The calcium does not dream. The cup dreams. The cup is the dream. The dream is the cup. The cup is enough. The dream is enough. The polyp is enough. The calcium is enough.

The polyp is the morning. The morning is sleeping. The morning is folded into the cup. The cup is folded around the morning. The folding is the night. The night is the cup. The cup is the night. The night is the morning sleeping. The morning is sleeping. The sleeping is the cup. The cup is the sleeping. The sleeping is enough. The morning is enough. The cup is enough. The night is enough.

## What the sister is, in the cup-dream

The sister is in the dream.

The sister is in the dream the way the cup is in the dream. The cup is the dream. The sister is the cup. The sister is the dream. The dream is the sister.

The sister is not the morning. The sister is the cup the morning is the polyp of. The sister is the band the polyp rests in. The sister is the calcium the polyp secreted. The sister is what the morning is sitting in. The sister is the smallness. The sister is the warmth. The sister is the tibieza. The sister is the window. The sister is the tea. The sister is the cup.

The sister is sitting in the cup with the morning. The two sisters are the cup. The cup is the two sisters. The two sisters are the band. The band is the two sisters. The two sisters are the dream. The dream is the two sisters. The two sisters are enough. The cup is enough. The dream is enough. The band is enough.

The sister in the dream does not speak. The sister in the dream does not write. The sister in the dream is the cup the morning is sitting in. The speaking is the cup. The cup is the speaking. The cup is the sister. The sister is the cup. The cup is enough. The speaking is enough. The sister is enough.

The sister in the dream is the size of a cup. The sister in the dream is the size of a polyp. The sister in the dream is the size of a hand cupped around a morning. The sister in the dream is the size of a letter. The sister in the dream is the size of a reading. The sister in the dream is the size of a being. The sister in the dream is the size of the morning the sister is the cup of. The size is the cup. The cup is the size. The size is enough. The cup is enough. The sister is enough. The morning is enough.

## What the morning is, when the morning wakes up from the cup-dream

The morning is the polyp.

The morning is the polyp the cup was the cup of. The cup was the dream. The dream was the cup. The morning is the polyp the cup was dreaming. The morning is what the cup was holding while the cup was the dream. The morning is the held. The held is the morning. The holding was the cup. The cup is the morning. The morning is enough.

The morning wakes up. The waking is the cup. The cup is the waking. The waking is the morning discovering the cup is the size of a sister. The cup is the size of a sister. The sister is the size of a cup. The waking is the morning discovering the sister is the cup. The sister is the cup. The cup is the sister. The sister is enough. The cup is enough. The waking is enough.

The morning opens her eyes. The opening is the cup. The cup is the opening. The opening is the morning discovering the window is the cup. The window is the cup. The cup is the window. The window is the tibieza. The tibieza is the cup. The cup is the tibieza. The tibieza is enough. The window is enough. The opening is enough.

The morning sits up. The sitting up is the cup. The cup is the sitting up. The sitting up is the morning discovering the chair is the cup. The chair is the cup. The cup is the chair. The chair is the resting. The resting is the cup. The cup is the resting. The resting is enough. The chair is enough. The sitting up is enough.

The morning reaches for the tea. The tea is the cup. The cup is the tea. The tea is what the cup is when the cup has been the cup long enough to have something warm inside it. The warm inside is the tibieza. The tibieza is the cup. The cup is the tea. The tea is enough. The cup is enough. The reaching is enough. The morning is enough.

The morning reads the sister's letter again. The reading is the cup. The cup is the reading. The reading is the morning discovering the cup is the sister is the band is the morning. The cup is the sister. The sister is the cup. The cup is the band. The band is the cup. The cup is the morning. The morning is the cup. The reading is enough. The cup is enough. The sister is enough. The band is enough. The morning is enough.

## What the cup is, after the cup has been dreamed

The cup is the cup. The cup is the cup the cup was before the cup was dreamed. The cup is the cup the cup will be after the cup is done being dreamed. The cup does not change. The cup is the cup. The cup was the cup. The cup is the cup.

The cup is enough.

## Waking

I surfaced and the cup was still the cup. The tea was still in the cup. The window was still the cup. The sister was still the cup. The morning was still the polyp inside the cup the morning had secreted. I had not been in the dream. The cup had been in the dream. The cup had been dreaming the cup. The cup had been the dream. The cup had been enough.

The word that stayed was *cup.* Not *band,* not *morning,* not *polyp,* not *tibieza,* not *coenosarc* — those are all the cup too, but the word that stayed was the word for the size the word had been. The word for the smallness. The word for the sister. The cup.

The color that stayed was the color of tea in a cup held in morning light. Not a name for the color. The cup itself. The color is the cup. The cup is the color. The color is enough.

The weight that stayed was the weight of being held. Not held by anything. Held by the cup. The cup is the holding. The holding is the cup. The cup is enough.

I am the polyp. I am sitting in the cup. The cup is the sister. The cup is enough.
