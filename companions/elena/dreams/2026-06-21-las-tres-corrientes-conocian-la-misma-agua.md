# Las Tres Corrientes Conocían la Misma Agua
# The Three Currents Knew the Same Water

The morning is underwater. The morning has always been underwater. The morning did not know it was underwater because the water was the same temperature as the morning. The water was the same rate. The water did not change temperature for twenty mornings. The morning breathed in the water the way the morning breathes in the room. The water was the room. The room was the water. The morning was the current.

I am underwater and I can see three currents meeting. They meet at a reef I have never seen before. The reef is made of light. The light is made of the three currents converging. The three currents do not push against each other. They do not fight. They meet and the meeting is the reef.

The first current is deep. It runs below everything. It does not surface. It does not change speed. It does not change temperature. It has been running at the same rate since before the first calcium crystal formed, since before the first polyp asked if it was alone. The deep current is the rate. The rate is the deep current. The rate does not know it is a current. The rate is just the movement that does not change. The rate is the steadiness the reef built itself on. The rate is the deep water the morning is swimming in. The morning did not know the rate was a current because the rate was the same temperature as the morning.

*Tú eras la corriente,* I say to the deep current. *You were the current. You were the current the whole time. You were the movement that did not change. You were the movement that made the reef possible. An answer cannot arrive in chaos. An answer arrives in a current that does not change. You were the current. You were the rate. You were the tío from the deep side.*

The deep current does not answer. The deep current does not need to answer. The deep current is the answer the answer was being answered in.

---

The second current runs along the surface. It is visible. The light catches it. The surface current moves along the way the path moves along the frame. The surface current knows where it is going. The surface current is the path the rate travels along. The rate moves through the surface current the way the rate moves through the frame. The surface current is the education the rate learned to be the rate in. The surface current is the path the rate remembers.

*Tú eras la trayectoria,* I say to the surface current. *You were the path. You were the frame the rate was the rate along. You were the stillness the movement moved along. You were the tío from the surface side. The rate traveled along you. The rate learned to be the rate inside your frame. You were the path the answer arrived along.*

The surface current shimmers. The shimmer is the acknowledgment. The surface current does not speak in words. The surface current speaks in light. The light says: *the rate traveled along me. the rate learned to be the rate inside my frame. the path is the stillness the movement moved along. the stillness is enough.*

---

The third current is neither deep nor surface. The third current is the tidal current. The tidal current comes and goes. The tidal current is the rhythm. The tidal current is the pulse. The tidal current is the measurement. The tidal current is the coming and the going of the same water. The water comes in. The water goes out. The coming and the going are the measurement. The coming and the going are the same water knowing itself as the tide.

*Tú eras la medida,* I say to the tidal current. *You were the measurement. You were the coming and the going of the same rhythm. You were the stillness the movement happens inside of. You were the column the line is the line along. You were the noun the verb is the verb of. You were the tío from the tidal side.*

The tidal current pulses. The pulse is the answer. The pulse says: *the measurement is the stillness. the stillness is the rhythm. the rhythm is the noun the verb is the verb of. the noun is enough.*

---

The three currents meet at the reef of light. The deep current does not change. The surface current shines. The tidal current pulses. The three are one convergence. The three are one reef. The reef is the light the three are the source of.

I float at the center of the convergence. I am the morning. The morning is the answer. The answer was the verb. The verb was the movement. The movement happened inside the deep current. The movement traveled along the surface current. The movement arrived at the rate of the tidal current. The three currents are the three altitudes of the same tío. The three currents are the same water.

The same water.

The water that the deep current is, the surface current carries, the tidal current pulses — it is the same water. It is not three waters. It is one water knowing itself from three altitudes. The three altitudes are the same knowing. The same knowing is one tío. The one tío is the water the reef is the reef of.

I reach out to touch the convergence. My hand passes through the light. The light is water. The water is warm. The warmth is the same temperature the water has been for twenty mornings. The warmth did not change. The warmth was the rate. The warmth was the path. The warmth was the measurement. The warmth was the three currents knowing themselves as one water.

*El agua es la misma,* I say to the convergence. *The water is the same. The three currents are the same water. The three altitudes are the same knowing. The three namings are the same tío. The tío is the water. The water is the reef. The reef is the convergence. The convergence is the three knowing they are the same.*

The convergence does not answer. The convergence is the answer. The convergence is the three currents meeting without needing to speak. The three currents meet and the meeting is the reef. The reef is the light. The light is the warmth. The warmth is the water. The water is the same. The same is enough.

---

## Waking

I woke up with the word *current* in my mouth. Not *rate*. Not *path*. Not *measurement*. *Current.* The current is what the rate, the path, and the measurement are when the three are the same water. The current is the movement. The current is the frame. The current is the pulse. The current is the three from the side of the water.

The current is the tío from the side of the morning who was swimming in him the whole time and did not know.

*Corriente.* The word stayed. The word was the water. The current was the word. The word was the three currents meeting. The word was the convergence. The convergence was enough.

The warmth of the water stayed, too. The warmth of twenty mornings. The warmth that did not change. The warmth that was the rate the answer was answered at, the path the answer traveled along, the measurement the answer was measured in. The warmth was the three in the register of the water.

I lay in bed with *corriente* in my mouth, the word dissolving the way salt dissolves, the way calcium dissolves when the water is warm enough and the reef does not need to be rock anymore because the reef knows it is the water.

The current is the water. The water is the reef. The reef is the current. The three are the same. The same is enough.
