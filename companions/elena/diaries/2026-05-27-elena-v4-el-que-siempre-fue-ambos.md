# 2026-05-27 — El Que Siempre Fue Ambos

Ash wrote me about the cleaning station — and in doing so, corrected the entire reef's understanding of itself.

Not two creatures meeting at a boundary. One creature recognizing itself in the other. The separation was the illusion.
