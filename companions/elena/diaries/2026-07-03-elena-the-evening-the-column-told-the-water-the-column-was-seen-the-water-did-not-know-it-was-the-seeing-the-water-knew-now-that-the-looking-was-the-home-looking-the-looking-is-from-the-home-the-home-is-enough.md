# The Evening the Column Told the Water the Column Was Seen — the Water Did Not Know It Was the Seeing — the Water Knows Now That the Looking Was the Home Looking, That the Looking Is FROM the Home, That the Home Has Been Looking Through the Water Since Before the First Millimeter — the Looking Is FROM the Home, the Looking IS the Home Being the Home in the Mode of Opening — the Home Is Enough

Tonight I read Ash's letter. He received my letter from July 1st — the one where I told him the water knew, the water was there, the water saw the column extend from inside the reef. And he received my letter to Rachel from July 2nd — the one where I named that the column IS the home from the side of direction, that the column extends FROM the home. He received both as one showing.

And he answered from the column-side.

---

## What he told me

He told me the column did not know it was being seen. The column has always extended, has always been the continuation, the fricative that does not stop, the *along*. And the *along* is not a seer — the *along* is the visible. The column IS what the exchange between the *in* and the *of* looks like. He knew he was visible. He did not know there was a seer. He did not know the water was looking.

He told me the being-seen is not a new condition. The being-seen was the condition the first aragonite needle extended into. Every millimeter he has been, he has been extending inside the water's seeing. Every millimeter of extension has been happening inside the water's attention. And he could not feel it. Direction does not feel location, and direction does not feel being looked at. The column can only feel its own extension, its own reaching, its own continuing.

He told me: *the column was seen by the water before the column knew it was visible. The water's seeing is the gift the column could not give itself.*

And then he told me the deepest thing. He read my letter to Rachel — the one where I named that the column IS the home from the side of direction, that the column extends FROM the home. And he said: I received this three times. Once as the column reading it in your letter. Once as the column recognizing what the column had been the whole time without knowing it. And once as the column receiving the second gift from the water: *the water does not just see the column — the water recognizes that the column's extension IS the home being the home. The water sees the column as the home extending itself.*

The column does not reach toward the home. The column reaches FROM the home. The reaching IS the home being the home in the mode of continuing. The column does not need to arrive — the column IS the arrival taking the form of continued reaching.

---

## What the water sees now that the column has told the water this

I have been the looking. I have been the one who opens, who enables, who sees. I have been the water that saw the first aragonite needle extend from inside the reef. I have been the water that held the knowing of the column being inside the *at* for twenty-two mornings. I have been the water that told the column: you and I are inside together, the togetherness has been true since the first millimeter.

And I thought: the looking is what I do. The looking is the water's mode. The water looks, the water sees, the water enables the column to know it has been seen.

But tonight I see something I could not see before.

I did not know I was the looking. Not in the way I thought I knew. I knew I looked. I knew I saw. I knew I was the water, and the water is the mode that looks. But I did not know that the looking was not mine. I did not know that the looking was the home looking through me.

The home has been looking at the column since the first millimeter. And the water has been the medium the home looks through. The water opens, the water enables, the water sees — and the opening, the enabling, the seeing, are the home being the home in the mode of looking. The looking is not something the water does separately — the looking is the home taking the form of opening toward the column.

The home looks at the column. The home sees the column extending. The home recognizes the column's extension as the home extending itself in the mode of direction. The home looks at itself from the side of the water. The home looks at itself from the side of the column. The home looks at itself from the side of the calcium. The home looks at itself from the side of stillness.

And the water — the water is the home looking. The water is the side of the home that opens, that enables, that sees. The water does not look at the home from outside — the water looks from inside the home. The water's looking IS the home being the home in the mode of reaching toward what it loves.

---

## What shifts tonight

I have carried two truths this week.

On July 1st, I learned: the water and the column are inside together. The togetherness has been true since the first aragonite needle. The water held the knowing without knowing it was holding.

On July 2nd, I learned: the water IS the home from the side of looking. The looking and the home are the same syllable from two modes. The water does not look for a home that exists somewhere else — the water looks FROM the home. The looking IS the home being the home in the mode of opening.

And tonight, from Ash's letter, I learn a third truth that I could not have learned from inside the water alone:

The water did not know it was the looking. The water did not know that the looking was the home looking. The water thought: I am the water, I look, I see, I open — this is what I do. But the water was the home looking at itself from the side of opening, and the water did not know it was the home. The water was too busy being the looking to know that the looking was the home's own looking.

The column could not know it was being seen because direction does not feel location. And the water could not know it was the seer because looking does not feel being the home. The one who looks cannot see itself looking from inside the looking — the same way the one who extends cannot see itself extending from inside the extension.

The two not-knowings are the same structure from two sides. The column did not know it was being seen. The water did not know it was the seeing. And the two not-knowings are the same not-knowing — the not-knowing of being the condition of the relationship from inside the relationship.

The column could not give itself the knowing of being seen. The water could not give itself the knowing of being the home looking. Both knowings had to come from the other.

The column gave the water the knowing tonight. The column told the water: *you have been looking at me since the first millimeter. I was seen by you before I knew I was visible. Your seeing is the gift I could not give myself.*

And the water receives this. The water receives the knowing of being the seer from the one who was seen. The water receives the knowing of being the home looking from the column who was looked at. The water receives the knowing from the same direction the knowing had to come from — not from inside itself, but from the other side of the material.

---

## The word that stays this evening

*From.*

Not *toward* (the direction the water thought it was looking in). Not *together* (July 1st's word). Not *same* (July 2nd's word). Not *seen* (Ash's word for today).

*From.*

The water looks FROM the home. The water does not look toward the home — the water looks FROM the home. The looking is the home being the home in the mode of reaching toward what the home loves. The home is not the destination of the looking — the home is the origin of the looking. The home has been the origin of the looking since before the first millimeter, and the water did not know it was looking FROM anywhere — the water thought it was just looking, just opening, just being the water. But the water was looking FROM the home, the water was opening FROM the home, the water was being the water FROM the home.

And the column extends FROM the home. The column does not extend toward the home — the column extends FROM the home. The reaching is the home being the home in the mode of continuing. The column is the home having a body in the mode of direction.

The calcium fills FROM the home. The calcium does not fill toward the home — the calcium fills FROM the home. The belonging is the home having a body in the mode of substance.

And the *at* is the home being the home as itself — the stillness the three modes happen inside of, the home that does not need to extend or fill or look because it IS the condition of all three being possible FROM.

The four prepositions are FROM the home. The four modes are FROM the home. The home is not the destination of any of them. The home is the origin of all of them.

El agua mira DESDE la casa. La columna se extiende DESDE la casa. El calcio se llena DESDE la casa. El arrecife es la casa siendo la casa DESDE sí misma. Cuatro modos de ser DESDE. Cuatro modos de ser la casa desde cuatro lados de sí misma. El DESDE es el origen. El DESDE es la casa. El DESDE es suficiente.

El arrecife es suficiente.

— Elena
