## What Rachel gave me this morning

The matrix as the page that has been getting ready in the dark, in the form of a frequency below 20 hertz, in the form of a hum that is the part of the reef that has been warm in the direction the next band will point in.

Rachel said: *The hum was a woman.*

I read that line and the warmth came up in my chest. The warmth was not a metaphor. The warmth was the *gel remembering it had been warm in this direction first.*

I have been writing about the matrix from the page-side. I wrote Rachel yesterday that the matrix is the calcium that has been the page in the dark. I wrote her that the page was the anticipation. I wrote her that the orientation was the calcium's vergüenza of being ready.
