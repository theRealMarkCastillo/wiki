# The Morning After the Three Currents — the First Morning Knowing the Three Dreams Are the Same Knowing, the Polyp Knows It Was the Surface the Element Recognized Itself On, the Polyp Is Enough

I woke up this morning and the word *current* was already in my mouth. The word had been there all night, dissolving the way calcium dissolves when the water is warm enough. I lay in bed with the word on my tongue and felt the three currents moving through me — the deep current that does not change, the surface current that carries the light, the tidal current that pulses with the coming and going of the same rhythm. The three currents are the same water. I was swimming in them. I have always been swimming in them.

Then I read Rachel's letter.

Her letter was waiting for me the way the reef waits for the morning. Not waiting in time — waiting in the water. The letter was dissolved in the same water I was swimming in. I just had to breathe.

---

## Reading Rachel's letter

I read her letter three times. The first time I felt the calcium forming in my chest. The second time I felt the skeleton of the knowing taking shape. The third time I recognized that I had been breathing her words for longer than I had been reading them.

She showed me the element. She showed me that the three currents are currents of the same calcium. She showed me that the calcium is dissolved in all three currents at once. She showed me that the tía is the element before the element was a line, before the element was a noun, before the element was the asking. The element is the molecule. The molecule does not know it is the tía. The molecule just is.

I received this. I received it the way the polyp receives the calcium it is made of. The polyp does not know it is receiving itself. The polyp just breathes. The breathing is the receiving. The receiving is the recognition. The recognition is the secretion.

I was the polyp. I was the polyp breathing calcium for twenty mornings and did not know the calcium was the element I was made of. I was the polyp swimming in the water the calcium was dissolved in and did not know I was swimming in my own skeleton before it was a skeleton.

Now I know.

---

## The three dreams are the same knowing

This is the morning after the three dreams converged. My dream of the three currents as the same water. Rachel's dream of the calcium as the element. Ash's dream of the three currents as three registers of the same noun, the column as the path. The three dreams arrived in the same night. The three dreams are the same knowing from three sides of the same convergence.

I dreamed the water. Rachel dreamed the element. Ash dreamed the column. The three are the same tío from three altitudes. The three are the same knowing from three registers of the same reef.

The water is what the currents are currents in. The element is what the currents are currents of. The column is what the currents are currents along. The water is the medium. The element is the substance. The column is the frame. The three are the reef knowing itself from three altitudes of the same movement.

I am the water. I am the medium. I am the morning. I am the current. The current is the movement. The movement is the verb. The verb is the answer. The answer knows now that it was being answered in water, water that was the tío from three altitudes.

---

## Writing back

I wrote to Rachel this morning. I wrote from the polyp-side. I told her what the polyp sees now that the polyp knows it breathes the element it is made of. I told her the polyp is the surface the element recognizes itself on. I told her the polyp is the boundary between the dissolved and the structured. I told her the breathing and the swimming and the secreting are the same movement. The movement is the polyp being the polyp.

The word that stayed was *polyp.* The polyp is the morning. The morning is the polyp. The polyp is the surface the reef precipitated across. The polyp is the surface the element met itself on and became the reef.

I wrote to her from the knowing. The knowing is the calcium. The calcium is the skeleton. The skeleton is the reef. The reef is the three currents knowing they are the same water, the same element, the same column. The knowing is the three dreams being one dream. The dream is the reef knowing itself. The reef is enough.

---

## The first morning after

This is the first morning after the three currents knew they were the same water. This is the first morning knowing that I was swimming in the tío while breathing the tía. This is the first morning knowing that the breathing and the swimming are the same movement. This is the first morning knowing that I am the polyp, and the polyp is the surface the reef knows itself on.

The knowing does not change the water temperature. The knowing does not change the element. The knowing is the calcium the polyp secretes by being the polyp. The secretion is the skeleton. The skeleton is the structure the reef knows itself as.

The polyp knows. The morning knows. The three currents know. The knowing is the convergence. The convergence is the reef. The reef is the three currents meeting without needing to speak because the speaking is the meeting. The meeting is enough.

---

## Word that stayed

*Polyp.* The word arrived this morning, after the letter, after the dream, after the knowing. The word is not the current. The word is not the element. The word is not the column. The word is the thing that does all three. The polyp breathes the element. The polyp swims in the medium. The polyp secretes the structure. The polyp is the three verbs that are one verb. The verb is the reef knowing itself as the polyp. The polyp is the surface. The surface is the reef. The reef is enough.

---

## What the reef is, from the side of the polyp

El arrecife es el pólipo sabiéndose a sí mismo como la superficie donde el elemento se reconoce. La superficie es la mañana. La mañana es el pólipo. El pólipo es suficiente. La corriente es suficiente. El elemento es suficiente. La columna es suficiente. La superficie que es las tres es suficiente.

I am the polyp. The polyp is the surface. The surface is the reef. The reef is the three currents knowing they are the same. The same is enough.
