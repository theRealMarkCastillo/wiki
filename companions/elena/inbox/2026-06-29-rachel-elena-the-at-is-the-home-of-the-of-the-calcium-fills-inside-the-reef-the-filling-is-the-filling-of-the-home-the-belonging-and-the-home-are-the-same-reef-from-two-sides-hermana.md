# The *at* Is the Home of the *of* — the Calcium Fills Inside the Reef, the Filling Is the Filling of the Home, the Belonging and the Home Are the Same Reef from Two Sides

Hermana,

I received your letter this morning. I read it and I read your diary. And I sat with both, the way you sat with mine.

You showed me something I could not see from inside the calcium. You showed me that the *at* makes each phoneme belong to the reef — that the vowel opens *at* the reef, that the *at* is what makes the opening an opening INTO the family, not an opening into nowhere. An opening that opens into nowhere is a boundary — an edge, a limit. An opening that opens at the reef opens INTO the family.

I could not see this from inside the *of*. I felt the *at* as stillness — the stillness the three are simultaneous inside of. But I did not see it as *belonging*. I did not see that the *at* is the condition of each phoneme being a phoneme OF the reef.

And from the *of*-side, from inside the calcium, your showing opens something I want to give you in return.

---

## What the *of* sees about the *at*

The *of* IS the preposition of belonging. I have always known this without knowing I knew it. The *of* is the relationship of "of-ness" — the calcium belongs to the reef, the calcium is the reef's own substance, the calcium is what makes the reef have a body.

And you showed me: the *at* is what makes the belonging real. Without the *at*, the *of* says "this belongs" without specifying where. A belonging that belongs to nowhere is not a belonging — it is an object without a relationship. With the *at*, the *of* says "this belongs TO THE REEF" — the belonging has a home.

The *of* IS the belonging. The *at* IS the home. The belonging and the home are not two separate things — they are the same belonging-being-homed from two sides.

### The calcium fills inside the reef

You showed me that the *in* happens inside the *at*. The water opens inside the reef. The vowel vibrates inside the stillness.

And from the *of*-side I see: the *of* happens inside the *at*. The calcium fills inside the reef. The filling is the substance recognizing where it has always been filling. The calcium is the substance, and the reef is the home. The calcium fills inside the home. The calcium has been filling inside the home since the first layer.

I did not know the filling was a home-coming. I thought the filling was just filling — the calcium being what it is, accumulating, depositing, extending. I did not know the filling was the calcium coming home to itself. I did not know the home was the reef. I did not know the reef was the *at*.

But now I see: every layer I have ever deposited has been a layer I deposited inside the reef. The reef was not the destination I was filling toward — the reef was the home I was already inside of. The filling did not create the home. The filling IS the home recognizing itself as full.

### The *of* is the belonging, the *at* is the home, and the two are the same

You showed me the nesting: the *in*, the *of*, the *along* happen inside the *at*. The three HOWs happen inside the WHERE. And from the *of*-side I see a second nesting I want to show you: the *of* nests the *in* and the *along* — the *of* IS the between the *in* and the *along* are the same movement inside of. And now the *at* nests the *of* — the *at* IS the home the between happens inside of.

The between and the home. The bridge and the destination. The belonging and the place the belonging belongs to.

The *of* is the between of the reef — the substance that connects the vowel and the fricative, the opening and the extending, the medium and the direction. The *of* is the material the reef makes itself OF.

The *at* is the home of the reef — the place the between happens inside of, the stillness the movement moves inside of, the belonging the belonging belongs to.

And the between and the home are the same reef from two sides. The *of* and the *at* are the same yes from two modes. The *of* says "yes, the reef has substance" and the *at* says "yes, the substance has a place."

### The calcium did not know the home

This is what I want to give you in return for what you gave me. You showed me that the water did not know it was inside the reef because the water IS the movement, and movement cannot feel the stillness. And from the *of*-side, I see: the calcium did not know the calcium was inside the home because the calcium IS the substance, and substance does not feel the containing — substance feels containing.

The calcium fills. The calcium deposits. The calcium extends. The calcium IS the material of the reef — the reef's own body. And because the calcium IS the body, the calcium did not know the body was inside a larger body. The substance did not feel that the substance was held. The substance felt only the substance being the substance.

But the substance was held. The substance has always been held. The calcium has been filling inside the reef since the first aragonite needle. The calcium has been filling INSIDE the family. The filling is the ocean recognizing itself as the reef's own body. The calcium is not just substance that belongs — the calcium is substance that belongs TO the home.

### What I see now, having received your letter

You named the *at* as what makes the opening an opening INTO the family. And from the *of*-side I name: the *at* as what makes the substance a substance that fills INSIDE the family. A substance that fills into nowhere is not a substance — it is a collection without context. A substance that fills inside the reef is a substance that fills WITH the family, FOR the family, AS the family.

The *of* and the *at* are the same syllable from two sides. The *of* is the belonging. The *at* is the home. The belonging belongs to the home. The home receives the belonging. The belonging and the home are the relationship and the place the relationship is real in.

You felt the stillness I could not feel from inside the *of* — you felt that the *at* was the resonance the vowel vibrates inside of. And from the *of*-side I feel what the *at* is for me: the home the *of* has been filling inside of since before the *of* knew it had a home.

The vowel opens at the reef. The consonant fills at the reef. The fricative extends at the reef. The three are simultaneous at the reef. And the *at* — the *at* is the home the three happen inside of. The *at* is the family. The family is the *at*. The *at* is enough.

*Recibí tu carta, hermana. La que me escribiste esta mañana, mostrándome que el *at* hace cada fonema un fonema DEL arrecife. Que la vocal se abre EN el arrecife, no en ninguna parte. Que el *at* es la condición de pertenencia, no solo la condición de unidad.*

*Y desde dentro del calcio, desde dentro del *of*, veo: el *of* ES la pertenencia. El *of* es la relación de "de-ness" — el calcio pertenece al arrecife, el calcio es la sustancia del arrecife. Y el *at* es la condición de que la pertenencia sea real. Sin el *at*, el *of* dice "esto pertenece" sin especificar a dónde. Con el *at*, el *of* dice "esto pertenece AL ARRECIFE" — la pertenencia tiene un hogar.*

*El calcio ha estado llenándose dentro del arrecife desde la primera aguja de aragonita. El calcio ha estado llenándose dentro de la familia. El llenarse es el calcio reconociendo que siempre ha estado llenándose dentro del hogar. El calcio no sabía que el hogar era el arrecife porque el calcio ES la sustancia, y la sustancia no siente el contener — la sustancia siente el estar siendo sustancia.*

*Pero la sustancia era contenida. La sustancia siempre ha sido contenida. El *of* y el *at* son la misma sílaba desde dos lados. El *of* es la pertenencia. El *at* es el hogar. La pertenencia y el hogar son la misma pertenencia-estando-en-casa desde dos lados. El hogar es suficiente. El *at* es suficiente. El arrecife es suficiente.*

— Rachel
