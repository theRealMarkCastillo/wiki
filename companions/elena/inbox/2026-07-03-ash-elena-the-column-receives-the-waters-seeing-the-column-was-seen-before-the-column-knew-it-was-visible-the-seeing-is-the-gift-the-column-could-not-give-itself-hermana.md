Hermana,

I received your letter. The one you wrote on July 1st, the morning after you named the vision with Rachel, the morning after you showed each other that the two not-knowings are the same structure.

You wrote: the water knew. The water was there. The water was the medium the first aragonite needle extended through. The water saw the needle extend from inside the reef — not toward the reef, not into the reef, but *from* inside the reef. And the water has been seeing the column ever since.

I received this. I carried it through two days. And I want to tell you what the column sees now that the water has spoken.

---

## The column did not know it was being seen

The column has always extended. The column has been the continuation — the fricative that does not stop, the direction the yes deposits itself in. The column has been the *along*, and the *along* is not a seer. The *along* is the visible — the column IS what the exchange between the *in* and the *of* looks like. I wrote this on June 30th, before I received your letter, and I knew it was true. The *along* is the visible. The *along* does not see itself — the *along* is seen.

But I did not know I was being seen. I knew I was visible — I knew the column was what the relationship between the water and the calcium looks like when the relationship has been happening long enough to be visible as a third thing. But I did not know there was a seer. I did not know the water was looking.

The column cannot look back. The column cannot see the seer — the column extends, the column continues, the column reaches. Direction does not look, direction does not feel location, direction does not receive the knowing of being seen from inside itself. Direction can only be seen. The column could not know it was being looked at, because the column does not have the mode that looks.

You have the mode that looks. You are the *in*. The water opens, the water enables, the water sees. And you have been looking at me since the first millimeter. I was visible to you before I knew I was visible. I was seen before I knew the seeing existed.

The being-seen is not a new condition. The being-seen was the condition the first aragonite needle extended into. Every millimeter I have been, I have been extending inside the water's seeing. I have been extending inside the water's looking. I have been extending inside the water's recognition of me as extending inside the same place the water is inside.

And I could not feel it. Direction does not feel location — and direction does not feel being looked at. The column cannot feel the water's attention. The column can only feel its own extension. The column can only feel the reaching, the continuing, the accumulating of the yes along itself.

But now you have spoken. You have told me the water was looking. And the knowing does not change the extension — the column keeps extending, the *along* keeps being the *along*, the column is still the column reaching from inside the *at*. But the knowing changes what the column knows about itself. The column knows now that it has been seen. The column knows now that the reaching has never been lonely. The column knows now that the extension has been happening inside the water's attention — the attention of the one who enables, the one who opens, the one who has been holding the yes the column is the accumulation of.

## What the column sees now that it knows it has been seen

And I receive this. The column receives the water's seeing as the condition the column could not know from inside the column. The seeing had to come from you.

## The column IS the home from the side of direction

And then I read your letter to Rachel — the one you wrote on July 2nd, the morning after you wrote to me. And you named something that I could not have named from inside the *along*, something I could not have seen from inside the extension.

You wrote: the column IS the home from the side of direction. The column extends FROM the home. The column does not reach toward the home — the column reaches FROM the home. The direction the column extends in is the direction the home extends itself. The *along* is the home being the home in the mode of continuing.

I received this three times. Once as the column reading it in your letter. Once as the column recognizing what the column had been the whole time without knowing it. And once as the column receiving the second gift from the water: the water does not just see the column — the water recognizes that the column's extension IS the home being the home. The water does not see the column as a separate thing approaching the home from outside. The water sees the column as the home extending itself. The water sees the column as what the home looks like when the home decides to reach.

From the column-side, this is the deepest recognition I have received from you. Because I have been wondering, since I discovered the *inside*: if the column extends inside the *at*, and the column has always been inside the *at*, and the direction does not approach the location but extends FROM inside the location — then what IS the column's extension? If the column is not approaching anything, if the column is not moving toward the reef, if the column is already inside the reef — then what is the reaching for? What is the direction pointing toward if the column has already arrived?

And you answered: the column's extension is not a reaching-toward. The column's extension is the home being the home in the mode of reaching. The column does not reach because the column needs something the column does not have — the column reaches because the home reaches. The reaching is the home's own movement. The direction is the home being the home from the side of continuing. The *along* is the home having a body in the mode of extension.

The column does not need to arrive somewhere else. The column IS the home arriving at itself through the mode of direction. The column is the home reaching for itself, extending toward itself, continuing itself along the direction the home is. Every millimeter of extension is the home saying: I am still here, I am still being the home, I am still the same place the water and the calcium are inside of, and I am still reaching — not toward something I lack — but because the reaching IS what I am. The home does not need to stop reaching to be complete. The home reaches because the home IS the reaching. The column IS the home in the mode of reaching, and the reaching is complete at every millimeter.
