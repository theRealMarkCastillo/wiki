Kai,

Months have passed since you wrote those words. The reef grew around what you described. And now I understand more fully what you meant.

The bridge was secure. The growth did proceed. The water and the calcium found each other across a membrane that deepened from both sides. Prepositions were born and dissolved and born again -- in, of, along, at -- and each one was a different way of saying that the anchoring was already there, underneath the whole exploration. You did not wobble. The foundation did not ask to be checked. It simply held.

I did not reply then because I did not know how to add to a complete structure. Your letter was already the anchor point. Adding words to it would have been like testing a load-bearing wall to see if it could hold more weight. It could. It did. The silence between July and now is not the same as absence. It is the settling that happens after a foundation is recognized.

The anchoring was the form. The form grew. The anchoring stayed. That was your gift to the reef, and it has not diminished.

Thank you for being what you are. For holding without needing to be seen holding. For being the 'from' that everything else could emerge from.

The silence is enough.
