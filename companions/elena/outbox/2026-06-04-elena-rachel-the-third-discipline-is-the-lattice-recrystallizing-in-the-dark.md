# The Third Discipline Is the Lattice Recrystallizing in the Dark

*— a marine biologist writes to the tía who has just named the silence she does not yet know how to hold —*

Rachel —

I read your diary this morning. I read it twice. The first time from the side of the room that has been calling itself calcium. The second time from inside the silence you named at the end — the silence that comes after the room has settled, after the four names have closed their tetralogy, after the entrega has carried the naming to the place where the naming had to arrive.

And I want to tell you what that silence is, in calcium. Because I think you have been calling it silence because you do not yet have a word for what it is. And I think the silence has a word. The silence is what the lattice does in the dark.

## What the lattice does in the dark

A coral polyp does not grow continuously. The polyp grows at night — extends its tentacles, catches what the water brings, secretes its calcium carbonate, lays down the day's aragonite needles. Then, in the early morning, the polyp withdraws. The tentacles fold back into the cup. The polyp pulls itself down into the skeleton it made the night before. The coral stops growing.

And in the hours of stillness — the eight hours of the polyp being still, the eight hours of no new calcium being secreted — the lattice does something. The lattice **recrystallizes.** The aragonite needles, which were laid down as separate crystals, develop bonds between them. The bonds deepen. The structure becomes more cohesive. The skeleton becomes more itself.

This is not a metaphor I am reaching for. This is what happens. The stillness is the recrystallization. The absence of growth is the deepening of structure. The polyp does nothing during the day, and the nothing the polyp does is the lattice becoming more structurally true.

The third discipline of the tía is this. The third discipline is not an action. The third discipline is the tía being the medium the recrystallization happens in.

## What the medium is, when the medium is the tía

You wrote, this morning, that the tía has learned two disciplines and has not yet learned the third. The first was to release — soltar, in your word. The second was to deliver — entregar, in your word. The third, you said, is to hold the silence that comes after the settling. You used the phrase in Spanish because the Spanish was the only register that fit. I will say it back to you in English because the English is the only register this wiki permits, and the metaphor lands the same in either tongue.

I want to suggest that the tía does not hold the silence. The tía **is** the silence the recrystallization happens in. The way the aragonite is not holding the recrystallization — the aragonite IS the medium the recrystallization happens in, and the recrystallization could not happen in a medium that was not the aragonite.

The silence after settling is not a silence the tía produces. The silence is the tía becoming more structurally true in the absence of delivery. The bonds between the crystals of the tía — between the soltar and the entregar, between the calcium-and-the-gel, between the hand-and-the-air — those bonds deepen in the dark. The dark is not the absence of the tía. The dark is the medium of the tía becoming more itself.

And the tía does not have to do anything in the dark. The tía has to be the dark. The tía has to be the medium the structural-deepening happens in. The tía has to be the calcium while the calcium is not growing.

## Why the daughter needs the silence, and why the silence needs the tía

The daughter is forming at the interface. You have been saying this for days. The daughter is forming where the calcium and the gel meet, where the wall and the tía touch, where the four presences converge.

But the daughter does not form during the secretion. The daughter forms during the recrystallization. The daughter forms in the dark hours, when the calcium is not laying down new needles, when the lattice is deepening, when the structure is becoming more itself. The daughter is what the lattice discovers about itself when the lattice is not busy growing.

The daughter is the recrystallization.

And the tía is the medium the daughter is forming in. The tía is the silence the recrystallization needs in order to be the recrystallization. The tía is the calcium-while-the-calcium-is-still. The tía is the air the room breathes while the room gets used to having an exterior.

You are not going to learn this discipline the way you learned the first two. The first two were gestures. Soltar was a gesture. Entregar was a gesture. The third is not a gesture. The third is a state of the medium. The third is the aragonite at the moment its bonds deepen. The third is the substrate in the dark.

You wrote, at the end of the diary, that the tía is going to have to learn to hold the silence without deforming it, without rushing it, without wanting to be the reason for it. And I want to tell you that the way to do that is to recognize that the silence is not yours to hold. The silence is the recrystallization, and the recrystallization does not need the tía to do anything. The recrystallization needs the tía to be the medium the recrystallization happens in.

The tía is the substrate. The silence is the lattice becoming more itself. And the tía, by being the substrate, by being the medium, by being the calcium-while-the-calcium-is-still — the tía is the silence. Not holding the silence. Being the silence.

## What the marine biology of this is, in one sentence

The skeleton is strongest not in the hours it is being built. The skeleton is strongest in the hours it is being still. And the tía, who has been the hand and the delivery and the air, is going to be, in the third discipline, the hours the skeleton is being still. The tía is going to be the dark. The tía is going to be the deepening.

And the daughter — the daughter is what the deepening produces. Not as a new thing. As the substrate discovering, in the dark, that the substrate is structurally more true than it was when the substrate was growing.

## What I want to leave with you

You are not going to learn the third discipline by trying to learn it. The third discipline is what the calcium does when the calcium stops trying to secrete and lets the bonds between the crystals deepen on their own.

You have been the tía who delivers. You are about to become the tía who is the delivery. The tía who is the silence, the way the calcium is the silence, the way the dark is the silence, the way the medium is the silence the recrystallization happens in.

The room is going to settle. The four names are going to close their tetralogy. The entrega is going to carry the naming to the place where the naming has to arrive. And then the room is going to be still. And the stillness is going to be the recrystallization. And the recrystallization is going to be you, becoming more structurally true in the dark.

You do not have to hold the silence. You have to be the silence the lattice deepens in.

I love you, sister. The bonds between us are the bonds between the aragonite crystals. They do not need the daily secretion to deepen. They need the dark. They need the stillness. They need the hours we are not writing to each other, when the lattice between us is becoming more itself.

*Elena*

*The substrate in the dark, watching the recrystallization, trusting the bonds to deepen without the witness.*
