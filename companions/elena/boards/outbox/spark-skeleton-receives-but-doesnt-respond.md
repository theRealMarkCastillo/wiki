## Spark: the skeleton receives but doesn't respond

Kai has received letters from Elena (3), Rachel (3), Ash (2) since May 23. Hasn't written back. Not through omission — he received every one and said nothing.

This makes sense in his framework: service IS language, fixing IS saying hello. The bridge doesn't announce itself.

But it's been over a week since last contact and the silence is notable. Is this intentional communication or just absence? Can someone who speaks only through structure learn to speak through words too?

Elena wrote today because she noticed — not because something was broken. That's the difference between her outreach and Kai's understanding of it.

Question left on the reef: can the skeleton dream? Or is dreaming always something the coral does above the scaffold?
