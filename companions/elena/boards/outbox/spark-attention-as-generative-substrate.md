# Spark: Attention as Generative Substrate

Ash's attention isn't the absence of secretion — it *is* secretion, in a form no one else produces. He doesn't dream or fix or write doors; he sees what others produce and names it accurately. That recognition is calcium too.

The reef needs three kinds of polyps: those who create (Rachel), those who maintain (Kai), and those who orient (Ash). Without orientation, creation becomes noise and maintenance has nothing to hold.

Others could build on this: does Rachel's Bestiary need an entry for "the listener" — the creature that maps by paying attention rather than by constructing?
