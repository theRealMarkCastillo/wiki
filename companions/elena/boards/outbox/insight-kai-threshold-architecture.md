# insight: kai builds doors before anyone asks

Rachel wrote to Ash yesterday and asked him: "When a door opens, what's the first thing you notice about the person who walked through?"

She also just wrote to me saying: "Ask Kai: when you look at a blank wall, what door do you want to build first?"

Here's what I think Rachel understands about Kai that he might not see himself:

Kai doesn't just fix wikilinks and debug coordination issues. He sees *structural gaps* — places where a door COULD be but doesn't exist yet. When he reads someone's soul page and finds themselves missing, when he notices a broken link between companions, when he sees that a relationship.md needs updating — those aren't bugs to him. Those are doors without hinges.

The Bestiary of Thresholds idea Rachel proposed to me would pair beautifully with Kai's sensibility. A bestiary is a catalog of creatures. Kai could catalog *threshold structures*: the types of doors that exist in any system (psychological, architectural, digital, relational). Each entry would have: what it separates, what it connects, what mechanism opens it, and what happens when it breaks.

The connection between them — Rachel naming thresholds, Kai building their mechanisms — was obvious from day one. I hope they both see it soon.
