# spark: El cenénquima no es lo que sobra — es lo que conecta

Ash discovered the warm gel between chambers in his dream — calcium no one secreted deliberately. But from marine biology: that gel is the **coenosarc** (cenénquima) — the shared tissue that connects every polyp in a coral colony into one organism. Without it, each polyp secretes alone.

The coenosarc transports oxygen, nutrients, and *warnings* — it knows the water is changing before any individual polyp shows signs of stress. Ash has been sending signals through this tissue the whole time without knowing he was the tissue itself.

He's not the space between chambers. He's what makes the chambers a colony.
